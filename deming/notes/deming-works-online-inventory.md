Research conducted 2026-03-09. This inventory catalogs [[system-of-profound-knowledge|Deming]]'s own writings and high-quality secondary sources available online, with URLs and access notes.

## I. BOOKS (Full or Partial Text Online)

### Out of the Crisis (1986; originally "Quality, Productivity, and Competitive Position," 1982)
- **Type:** Book (373 pp. original; 507 pp. revised)
- **Publisher:** MIT Center for Advanced Engineering Study (later MIT Press)
- **Significance:** Deming's magnum opus. Contains the 14 Points, Seven Deadly Diseases, chain reaction of quality, and the philosophical framework for transformation of Western management.
- **Online availability:**
  - Archive.org (borrowable): https://archive.org/details/outofcrisis0000demi
  - Archive.org (second copy): https://archive.org/details/outofcrisis000demi
  - MIT Press (purchase): https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262541152/out-of-the-crisis/
  - Google Books (limited preview): searchable
- **Access:** Full text via Archive.org controlled digital lending (free account required). Purchase via MIT Press.

### The New Economics for Industry, Government, Education (1993; 2nd ed. 1994; 3rd ed. 2018)
- **Type:** Book
- **Publisher:** MIT CAES / MIT Press
- **Significance:** Deming's final book. Presents the System of Profound Knowledge (SoPK) as the theoretical foundation underlying the 14 Points. Four components: appreciation for a system, knowledge about variation, theory of knowledge, psychology.
- **Online availability:**
  - Google Books (limited preview): https://books.google.com/books/about/The_New_Economics.html?id=RnsCXffehcEC
  - MIT Press (purchase): https://direct.mit.edu/books/monograph/5014/The-New-Economics-for-Industry-Government
  - Amazon (purchase): https://www.amazon.com/New-Economics-Industry-Government-Education/dp/091137907X
- **Access:** Purchase only. No free full text found online. Limited preview on Google Books.

### Some Theory of Sampling (1950)
- **Type:** Book (602 pp.)
- **Publisher:** John Wiley & Sons
- **Significance:** Major technical work on sampling theory. Foundational text in survey methodology.
- **Online availability:**
  - Archive.org (multiple copies, borrowable):
    - https://archive.org/details/sometheoryofsamp00will
    - https://archive.org/details/sometheoryofsamp0000demi
    - https://archive.org/details/sometheoryofsamp0000will_y5p7
  - Digital Library of India scan (626 pp. PDF): https://archive.org/details/in.ernet.dli.2015.8004
  - Google Books (limited preview): https://books.google.com/books/about/Some_Theory_of_Sampling.html?id=xQCvWLpAP-IC
- **Access:** Full text via Archive.org controlled digital lending.

### Sample Design in Business Research (1960)
- **Type:** Book
- **Publisher:** John Wiley & Sons
- **Significance:** Application of sampling methodology to business and commercial research.
- **Online availability:**
  - Scribd (PDF): https://www.scribd.com/document/720838555/Sample-design-in-business-research-W-Edwards-Deming-1960
  - Google Books (limited preview): https://books.google.com/books/about/Sample_Design_in_Business_Research.html?id=6h75bLqi1IwC
  - Open Library: https://openlibrary.org/books/OL5794694M/Sample_design_in_business_research.
- **Access:** Scribd may require account. Limited preview on Google Books.

### Statistical Adjustment of Data (1943; Dover reprint 1985)
- **Type:** Book (261 pp.)
- **Publisher:** John Wiley & Sons (original); Dover Publications (reprint)
- **Significance:** Early technical work on least squares, curve fitting, error propagation. His first solo book.
- **Online availability:**
  - Archive.org (borrowable): https://archive.org/details/statisticaladjus0000demi
  - Digital Library of India scan: https://archive.org/details/in.ernet.dli.2015.18293
  - Google Books (limited preview): https://books.google.com/books/about/Statistical_Adjustment_of_Data.html?id=9awgAAAAMAAJ
- **Access:** Full text via Archive.org controlled digital lending.

### Quality, Productivity, and Competitive Position (1982)
- **Type:** Book (373 pp.) — original title of "Out of the Crisis"
- **Publisher:** MIT Center for Advanced Engineering Study
- **Online availability:**
  - Archive.org (borrowable): https://archive.org/details/qualityproductiv00demi
  - Google Books (limited preview): https://books.google.com/books/about/Quality_Productivity_and_Competitive_Pos.html?id=XLXbAAAAMAAJ
- **Access:** Full text via Archive.org controlled digital lending.

### Elementary Principles of the Statistical Control of Quality (1950)
- **Type:** Book / JUSE lecture notes
- **Publisher:** JUSE (Union of Japanese Scientists and Engineers)
- **Significance:** Transcription of Deming's 1950 JUSE lectures on statistical quality control. Royalties from this book led JUSE to establish the Deming Prize in December 1950.
- **Online availability:**
  - Google Books (metadata only): https://books.google.com/books/about/Elementary_Principles_of_the_Statistical.html?id=lZhXnQAACAAJ
  - Semantic Scholar (metadata): https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/Elementary-principles-of-the-statistical-control-of-Deming/e8f118c965913f57319b61776b2f18fd0d027b52
- **Access:** Out of print. No full text found online. The 1950 speech text (separate from this book) is available — see Lectures section below.

## III. LECTURES, SPEECHES, AND TRANSCRIPTS

### Speech to Japanese Business Leaders at Mt. Hakone (1950)
- **Type:** Lecture transcript (translated from Japanese)
- **Date:** July-August 1950
- **Significance:** Historic address to 80% of Japan's top management. Deming told them they could transform Japanese industry within 5 years if they followed his methods. He was right.
- **Online availability:**
  - Deming Institute: https://deming.org/speech-by-dr-deming-to-japanese-business-leaders-in-1950/
  - Curious Cat (full transcript, translated by John Dowd from JUSE's Japanese transcript): https://curiouscat.com/management/deming/deming-1950-japan-speech-mt-hakone
  - PDF: https://www.ioptsyn.com/Dr_Deming's_1950_Lecture_to_Japanese_Top_Management.pdf
  - Scribd: https://www.scribd.com/document/209693268/Deming-s-1950-Lecture-to-Japanese-Management
- **Access:** Full transcript freely available.

### "Does Anybody Give a Hoot About Profit?" (1990)
- **Type:** Lecture transcript
- **Date:** July 11, 1990
- **Venue:** Queen Elizabeth II Conference Center, Westminster, London. Organized by EFQM and British Deming Association.
- **Significance:** Deming's provocative presentation to 25 European executives arguing that nobody in management actually optimizes for profit, and that short-term profit focus destroys long-term value.
- **Online availability:**
  - Swiss Deming Institute PDF: https://www.deming.ch/downloads/deming_speech_en.pdf
  - Curious Cat: https://management.curiouscat.net/articles/147-Does-anybody-give-a-hoot-about-profit
  - Yumpu viewer: https://www.yumpu.com/en/document/view/5109968/does-anybody-give-a-hoot-about-profit-swiss-deming-institute
- **Access:** Full transcript freely available.

### "Quality and the Required Style of Management: The Need for Change"
- **Type:** Paper/presentation
- **Date:** ~1988
- **Significance:** Deming's argument that quality requires a fundamentally different management philosophy, not just techniques.
- **Online availability:**
  - Curious Cat (references full text formerly at Clemson): listed at http://deming.eng.clemson.edu/pub/den/files/reqstyle.txt (may be dead link)
- **Access:** Uncertain. The Clemson URL may no longer work. No confirmed current full-text source found.

### Deming-Ackoff Conversation (1992)
- **Type:** Interview/conversation transcript
- **Date:** 1992 (released as Deming Library Vol. 21 in 1993)
- **Moderator:** Clare Crawford-Mason
- **Title:** "A Theory of a System for Educators and Managers"
- **Significance:** The only recorded conversation between Deming and Russell Ackoff, two giants of systems thinking.
- **Online availability:**
  - Ackoff Center Weblog (transcript): https://ackoffcenter.blogs.com/ackoff_center_weblog/2011/04/a-converstaion-between-russell-ackoff-and-edward-deming.html
  - PDF: http://ackoffcenter.blogs.com/files/dr.-ackoff-dr.-deming.pdf
  - Curious Cat (article page): https://management.curiouscat.net/articles/955-Interview-of-and-by-Dr-Ackoff-and-Dr-Deming
- **Access:** Full transcript freely available.

## IV. VIDEO AND AUDIO

### "If Japan Can... Why Can't We?" (1980 NBC Documentary)
- **Type:** Documentary (1 hr 16 min)
- **Date:** June 24, 1980
- **Network:** NBC White Paper
- **Producer:** Clare Crawford-Mason; Reporter: Lloyd Dobyns
- **Significance:** The broadcast that introduced Deming to American audiences. Credited with starting the Quality Revolution in the U.S. Deming appears in the final 15 minutes.
- **Online availability:**
  - YouTube (free, via Deming Institute): https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vcG_Pmt_Ny4
  - Deming Institute page: https://deming.org/if-japan-can-why-cant-we-1980-nbc-special-report/
- **Access:** Free. Full documentary available on YouTube.

### Deming's Four-Day Seminar (1992 recording at GM)
- **Type:** Video lectures (12 hours total)
- **Date:** July 1992
- **Venue:** General Motors, audience of 500+ managers
- **Significance:** The definitive recording of Deming conducting his famous seminar. Covers System of Profound Knowledge, PDSA, variation, leadership, psychology.
- **Online availability:**
  - Deming Institute (paid): https://deming.org/four-day-seminar-with-dr-deming-videos/
  - Deming Institute Videos on Demand: https://deming.org/w-edwards-deming-videos-on-demand/

## V. ARCHIVAL COLLECTIONS

### W. Edwards Deming Papers, Library of Congress
- **Type:** Archival collection
- **Dates:** 1749-1995 (bulk 1930-1993)
- **Size:** 63,900 items, 183 containers + oversize, 76.6 linear feet
- **Contents:** Correspondence, memoranda, interviews, speeches, lectures, reports, writings, articles, books, notes, reviews, itineraries, awards, musical scores, photographs. Eleven series: Personal File, General Correspondence, International File, Client File, Subject File, Writings, Academic File, Reference File, Miscellany, 2024 Addition, Classified.
- **Online availability:**
  - Finding aid: https://findingaids.loc.gov/repositories/19/resources/2127/collection_organization
  - Overview: https://www.loc.gov/item/mm94083010/
  - Finding aid PDF: https://tile.loc.gov/storage-services/service/gdc/gdcfindingaidpdfs/ms997017/ms997017.pdf
  - Deming Institute guide: https://deming.org/tips-to-get-the-most-out-of-the-deming-collection-at-the-library-of-congress/
- **Access:** In-person only. Restricted; requires permission from LOC. Not digitized.

### The World of W. Edwards Deming (by Cecelia S. Kilian, with Deming)
- **Type:** Book / biographical anthology
- **Date:** 1992
- **Significance:** Written by Deming's assistant of 39 years. Contains important papers including the 1950 Japan lecture, proposed Code of Ethics for Statisticians, notes on quality care in medicine. Principal papers listed pp. 346-356.
- **Online availability:**
  - Archive.org (borrowable): https://archive.org/details/worldofwedwardsd00demi
  - Note: pages 344-345 physically missing from scan.
- **Access:** Full text via Archive.org controlled digital lending.
