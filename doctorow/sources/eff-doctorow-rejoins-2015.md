This January 2015 EFF press release announcing Doctorow's return to the [[electronic-frontier-foundation]] as Special Advisor is a key biographical document. Doctorow had previously worked at EFF from 2001 to 2006 as its European Affairs Coordinator; the 2015 rejoining marked a formal re-engagement with the organization that had defined his early career and that remains the institutional center of his activist work.

The framing of the announcement — "to eradicate DRM everywhere" — captures the specific mandate around which Doctorow returned. [[digital-rights-management-critique]] had been central to his work from the beginning: his early EFF years coincided with the post-DMCA debates over anti-circumvention law, and his persistent argument that DRM harms users, security researchers, and the public interest while protecting only incumbent distributors runs from his earliest writing through to [[the-internet-con]] and [[enshittification-book]].

The 2015 return coincided with a period of intensified DRM conflict: the W3C was debating whether to include DRM (Encrypted Media Extensions) in the HTML5 standard, a dispute in which EFF and Doctorow were centrally involved. The [[w3c]] EME controversy — in which the web standards body voted to include DRM in the open web platform over EFF's objection — led to Doctorow's public resignation from the W3C Advisory Committee in 2017, one of the more dramatic institutional confrontations of his career.

This source documents the institutional context for Doctorow's most productive policy period. His EFF affiliation provides the organizational infrastructure — legal resources, lobbying capacity, public credibility — within which his concepts like [[adversarial-interoperability]] and [[competitive-compatibility]] were developed and deployed as policy arguments rather than merely as analytical observations. The "eradicate DRM everywhere" mandate also shows that the framework he would later call enshittification — the use of legal and technical lock-in to trap users and suppress competition — was already the animating concern of his work a full eight years before he named it.
