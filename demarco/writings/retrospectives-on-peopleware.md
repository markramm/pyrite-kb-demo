"Retrospectives on Peopleware" was a panel at the 29th International Conference on Software Engineering (ICSE 2007), featuring DeMarco, [[timothy-lister]], [[fred-brooks]], [[edward-yourdon]], Linda Rising, and [[barry-boehm]], moderated by Steven Fraser. The panel reflected on the two-decade impact of [[peopleware]] on the software engineering field.

The event is significant as a rare occasion when DeMarco and [[timothy-lister]] appeared alongside their intellectual predecessors and contemporaries — [[fred-brooks]] (whose *Mythical Man-Month* was the earlier organizational critique), [[edward-yourdon]] (DeMarco's structured methods mentor), and [[barry-boehm]] (the plan-driven tradition's champion) — to assess what [[peopleware]] had and had not achieved. This retrospective foreshadowed DeMarco's more radical self-critique in [[software-engineering-an-idea-whose-time-has-come-and-gone]] two years later.
