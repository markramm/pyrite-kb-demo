*Happy to Work Here: Understanding and Improving the Culture at Work* is the most recent collaborative work by the [[atlantic-systems-guild]] principals — DeMarco, [[timothy-lister]], [[peter-hruschka]], [[steve-mcmenamin]], and [[suzanne-robertson]]. The book extends the [[peopleware-thesis]] into a broader treatment of workplace culture, building on the decades of consulting experience that informed both [[peopleware]] and [[adrenaline-junkies-and-template-zombies]].

The title signals continuity with the humanistic tradition of [[peopleware]]: the argument that productive knowledge work depends on organizations creating conditions where people genuinely want to work, rather than extracting labor through the [[spanish-theory-of-management]].
