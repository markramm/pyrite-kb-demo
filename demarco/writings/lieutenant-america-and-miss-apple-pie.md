*Lieutenant America and Miss Apple Pie* (2003) is a collection of short stories by Tom DeMarco, published by Down East Books. Like [[dark-harbor-house]], it reflects DeMarco's Maine-based literary sensibility and his parallel career as a fiction writer alongside the technology management works.
