*A Ruby Beam of Light* (2016) is the first volume of Tom DeMarco's *Dark World Chronicles* series, followed by [[airship-nation]]. The premise is "gentle apocalyptic" — a mysterious disturbance in Earth's magnetic field inhibits all explosions, effectively repealing the technological foundations of the twentieth and twenty-first centuries.

The series represents DeMarco's most ambitious fiction project and continues the parallel literary career that began with [[dark-harbor-house]].
