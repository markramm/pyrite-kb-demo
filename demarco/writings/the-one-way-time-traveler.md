*The One-Way Time Traveler* is Tom DeMarco's speculative novel depicting a future society controlled entirely by women — described as a flip of Margaret Atwood's *The Handmaid's Tale*. The novel examines society's problems and inequalities through a cataclysmic lens.

Like [[dark-harbor-house]] and [[a-ruby-beam-of-light]], this novel belongs to DeMarco's parallel career as a fiction writer during the [[reflective-era]] of his intellectual development.
