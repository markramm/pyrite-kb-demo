The Cutter Consortium is a technology research and advisory firm where both DeMarco and [[timothy-lister]] served as Fellows. The consortium provided a platform for their consulting work and thought leadership beyond the [[atlantic-systems-guild]], connecting them to a broader community of technology management practitioners and researchers.

Lister served on the Cutter Business Technology Council and hosted several Cutter Summits, reflecting his role as a public voice for the [[peopleware-thesis]] in industry forums.
