[[waltzing-with-bears]] won the 2004 Jolt Award for best general computing book, the second Jolt Award for DeMarco and [[timothy-lister]]. The Jolt Awards, given by *Dr. Dobb's Journal*, recognized outstanding contributions to the software development field and were considered one of the most prestigious book awards in software engineering.

DeMarco had previously won the 1998 Jolt Award for [[the-deadline]]. The awards recognized both the literary and intellectual qualities of DeMarco's writing — his ability to present serious arguments about software management in accessible, engaging prose.
