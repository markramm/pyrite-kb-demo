Developer On Fire Episode 238, hosted by Dave Rael, features [[gerald-weinberg]] in conversation under the title "Human Tools." The episode was recorded in 2017, making it one of Weinberg's later public interviews, delivered in the period when his health was declining and [[don-gray]] had begun taking over [[psl-workshop-founded-1974]] facilitation.

The "Human Tools" framing reflects Weinberg's lifelong argument that software development's most important tools are human beings—their judgment, creativity, communication, and relationships. This theme runs from [[psychology-of-computer-programming-1971]] through [[becoming-a-technical-leader-1986]], [[secrets-of-consulting-1985]], and the [[quality-software-management-framework]], and it connects to the broader concept of [[programming-as-human-activity]].

The episode offers Rael's audience—primarily working software developers—an accessible introduction to Weinberg's ideas in his own voice. It complements the more technically focused [[leanpub-frontmatter-interview-2017]] and the career-spanning [[spamcast-grandfather-of-agile-2015]]. As a late-career primary source, it captures Weinberg's continued engagement with ideas about software development as a fundamentally human endeavor.
