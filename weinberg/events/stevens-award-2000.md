[[gerald-weinberg]] won the Stevens Award for Contributions to Software Engineering in 2000, recognizing his lifetime contributions. Named after Wayne Stevens, the award honors outstanding contributions to the methods of software development. By this point Weinberg had published over 30 books spanning psychology, systems thinking, consulting, leadership, and quality management.
