Esther Derby studied under [[gerald-weinberg]] and co-founded the [[aye-conference]] with Weinberg and [[johanna-rothman]]. She became a prominent voice in the Agile coaching and organizational development community, authoring "Agile Retrospectives" (2006) with Diana Larsen. Derby is one of the primary transmission channels through which Weinberg's ideas about [[congruent-behavior]], organizational dynamics, and the human side of software reached the Agile movement.
