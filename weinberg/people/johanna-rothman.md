Johanna Rothman studied under [[gerald-weinberg]] and co-founded the [[aye-conference]]. She became a prolific author on software project management ("Manage It!", "Behind Closed Doors" with [[esther-derby]]). Rothman extended Weinberg's management thinking into practical guidance for hiring, project management, and organizational design in software. Along with Derby, she is a key carrier of Weinberg's humanistic approach into contemporary software management.
