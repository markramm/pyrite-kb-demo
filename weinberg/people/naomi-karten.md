Naomi Karten is a consultant and author specializing in communication, change management, and service management in technology organizations. She is a long-standing member of the community that formed around [[gerald-weinberg]] and [[aye-conference]].

Karten co-edited [[amplifying-your-effectiveness-2000]] alongside Weinberg and [[james-bach]], a collected volume that brought together practitioners working in the tradition of humanistic, systems-oriented software development. Her own work on communication and organizational behavior reflects Weinberg's influence, particularly his emphasis on [[congruent-behavior]] and the interpersonal dimensions of technical work.

She is the author of several books on managing expectations, handling change, and improving communication in IT organizations—themes that parallel Weinberg's concerns in [[becoming-a-technical-leader-1986]], [[secrets-of-consulting-1985]], and the [[quality-software-management-framework]] series. Karten has been an active voice in the broader community of practice that Weinberg helped create.
