Ken Orr was an early colleague of [[gerald-weinberg]]'s in the structured methods era of software development. He developed the Orr methodology for data-driven design. His collaboration with Weinberg represents the period when Weinberg was active in the methodological debates of the 1970s-80s before fully committing to the human-centered approach that would define his later work.
