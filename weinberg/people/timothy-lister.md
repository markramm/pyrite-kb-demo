Timothy Lister co-authored "Peopleware" with [[tom-demarco]], contributing empirical research (Coding War Games) that validated many of [[gerald-weinberg]]'s intuitions about the importance of environment, team dynamics, and management style to software productivity. The Coding War Games data showed that environment and management practices mattered far more than individual talent — a finding entirely consistent with Weinberg's [[programming-as-human-activity]] thesis.
