Publisher of most of [[gerald-weinberg]]'s major works from the 1980s onward, including [[secrets-of-consulting-1985]], [[becoming-a-technical-leader-1986]], the four-volume [[quality-software-management-framework]], and the "Silver Anniversary" editions of [[psychology-of-computer-programming-1971]] and [[introduction-to-general-systems-thinking-1975]]. Dorset House specialized in software engineering and project management titles. [[wendy-eakin]] was editor at Dorset House and worked closely with Weinberg on several of these titles.
