Self-publishing platform where [[gerald-weinberg]] published numerous shorter works in his later career, including [[agile-impressions-2013]], novels, and essay collections. Weinberg's adoption of Leanpub reflected his entrepreneurial approach to publishing and his comfort with reaching audiences directly.
