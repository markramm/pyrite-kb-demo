IBM's Federal Systems Division where [[gerald-weinberg]] began his career in 1956. He managed operating systems development for NASA's [[project-mercury-1959|Project Mercury]], the first American human spaceflight program. The division's work on mission-critical government and military software provided Weinberg with his formative observations of programming teams under pressure.
