Software as a Human Activity Performed Effectively — a professional community that [[gerald-weinberg]] was involved with in his later years. The name itself encapsulates his life's thesis: [[programming-as-human-activity]]. SHAPE focused on the intersection of human effectiveness and software practice. The forum's online dialogues were collected into published volumes, including [[roundtable-on-project-management-2001]] and its companion [[roundtable-on-technical-leadership-2002]].
