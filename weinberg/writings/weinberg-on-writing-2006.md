Weinberg's book on writing methodology, introducing the [[fieldstone-method]]. Explains how he wrote 40+ books: by collecting ideas ("fieldstones") continuously and assembling them into structures rather than writing linearly from outline to draft. Useful as both a writing guide and a window into Weinberg's intellectual process.
