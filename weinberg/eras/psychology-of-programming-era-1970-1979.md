The decade when [[gerald-weinberg]] established his intellectual foundations. [[psychology-of-computer-programming-1971]] launched the field of programming psychology. [[introduction-to-general-systems-thinking-1975]] provided the theoretical framework that would underpin all his later work. The [[ethnotechnical-review-handbook-1979]] began codifying [[technical-reviews-and-walkthroughs]]. By the end of this decade, Weinberg had moved from IBM to independence, founding [[weinberg-and-weinberg]] as a consulting practice. His unique combination — programming experience, psychological insight, systems thinking — was established. The decade also produced [[structured-programming-in-plc-1973]] and [[high-level-cobol-programming-1977]], more technically oriented works that demonstrate the breadth of his output before his focus narrowed to the human dimensions of software.
