Weinberg's most prolific decade. Six major books in ten years, spanning problem definition ([[are-your-lights-on-1982]] with [[donald-gause]]), consulting ([[secrets-of-consulting-1985]]), leadership ([[becoming-a-technical-leader-1986]]), and systems design ([[general-principles-of-systems-design-1988]] with [[dani-weinberg]]). The decade also produced [[rethinking-systems-analysis-and-design-1982]] and [[understanding-the-professional-programmer-1982]], both published early in the decade. The encounter with [[virginia-satir]] in 1988 was pivotal — the Change Shop workshop conceived by Satir, Dani, and Jerry would transform his approach to organizational change. Satir's death in September 1988 meant Weinberg became one of the carriers of her ideas into the technology world.
