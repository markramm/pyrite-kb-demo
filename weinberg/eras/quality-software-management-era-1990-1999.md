The decade of Weinberg's magnum opus: the four-volume [[quality-software-management-framework]]. This was his most systematic work, integrating [[general-systems-thinking]], [[virginia-satir]]'s therapy models, and decades of consulting observation into a comprehensive framework for software quality management. Vol. 1 introduced [[cultural-patterns-of-software-organizations]], Vol. 2 addressed measurement, Vol. 3 applied [[congruent-behavior]], and Vol. 4 applied the [[satir-change-model]] to organizational transformation. The [[warnier-prize-1993|Warnier Prize]] in 1993 recognized his cumulative contributions. Published through [[dorset-house-publishing]].
