TOCICO — the Theory of Constraints International Certification Organization — is the principal professional body for TOC practitioners worldwide. It provides certification programs, hosts an annual international conference, and serves as the institutional home for the global TOC community that [[eliyahu-goldratt]]'s work created.

## Role in the TOC community

TOCICO maintains and transmits the body of knowledge Goldratt developed: the Five Focusing Steps, the Theory of Constraints Thinking Processes (evaporating clouds, current reality trees, future reality trees), the drum-buffer-rope scheduling method, and Critical Chain project management. Its certification program provides a structured pathway for practitioners to develop competence in TOC tools and methods.

The organization connects practitioners across industries: manufacturing (where TOC originated), healthcare, project management, education, and increasingly software and knowledge work. TOCICO's annual conference is the primary gathering point for serious TOC practitioners and researchers.

## Ching's position in the TOC community

Ching has presented at TOCPA (TOC Practitioners Alliance) events, including a 2018 webinar presenting the [[focccus-formula]]. This involvement situates Ching within the broader global TOC community, not only within the Agile-focused subset. While Ching's primary audience is software practitioners and Agile teams, his work is recognized within TOCICO circles as a legitimate extension of TOC principles into software domains.

This dual community membership — Agile Scotland and the TOCICO ecosystem — defines Ching's distinctive position: he is a TOC practitioner who speaks Agile, and an Agile practitioner who speaks TOC. TOCICO represents the upstream professional tradition that validates Ching's work from the TOC side, while [[lean-agile-scotland]] and [[agile-scotland]] represent the downstream community he is trying to reach.
