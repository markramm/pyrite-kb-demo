## Overview

"The Art of Strategy" by Erik Schon is a published book that explicitly connects Sun Tzu, Boyd, and Wardley as a strategic synthesis. The book maps the intellectual connections between Sun Tzu's five factors, Boyd's OODA loop, and Wardley's mapping framework, presenting them as a coherent strategic tradition.

## Significance

Schon's book is significant as a secondary source because it treats the Sun Tzu-Boyd-Wardley lineage as a deliberate intellectual tradition rather than a series of individual contributions. It provides a structured treatment of the connections that Wardley himself describes in talks and writing, and makes the case that these three frameworks form a cumulative body of strategic thought.

Source: Erik Schon, "The Art of Strategy" (Leanpub)
