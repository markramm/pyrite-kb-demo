## Overview

Map Camp is a community conference dedicated to Wardley Mapping practice. It brings together practitioners from technology, government, military, and business to share mapping experiences, case studies, and methodological developments.

## Significance

Map Camp represents the maturation of the Wardley Mapping community from an informal network of practitioners into an organized community with regular events. The conference provides a venue for the kind of peer exchange that drives framework development — practitioners sharing what works, what does not, and what needs to be refined.

## Research Status

Specific dates, locations, and frequency of Map Camp events need to be verified. The conference has been held in multiple locations and is organized by community members rather than by Wardley directly.
