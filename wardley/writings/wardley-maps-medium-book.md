## Overview

Wardley's primary written work is a book-length series published chapter-by-chapter on Medium, titled "Wardley Maps" (also referred to as the "Wardley Mapping book" or simply "the book"). It was published incrementally over 2016-2018, with 19 chapters covering the full framework from basic mapping concepts through evolution theory, doctrine, climate patterns, and gameplay.

## Structure

The book follows a narrative structure, walking the reader through the framework as Wardley developed it:
- Early chapters introduce the problem of strategic decision-making without maps
- Middle chapters develop the mapping technique, evolution axis, and value chain concepts
- Later chapters cover doctrine, climate patterns, and gameplay
- The book includes worked examples and case studies from Wardley's experience

## Publication Approach

The chapter-by-chapter Medium publication is characteristic of Wardley's approach to knowledge dissemination: open, incremental, and community-oriented. Rather than publishing through a traditional publisher, Wardley chose to release the work freely on Medium, consistent with his broader commitment to Creative Commons licensing (CC BY-SA 4.0).

This publication approach means the "book" exists as a series of blog posts rather than a single bound volume. Community members have compiled it into various formats (PDF, epub) and it has been translated by community volunteers.

## Significance

The Medium book is the most comprehensive single source for understanding Wardley's framework as articulated by Wardley himself. It serves a function similar to Boyd's "Patterns of Conflict" briefing — the definitive statement of the framework by its creator, published in an unconventional format that reflects the creator's values about knowledge dissemination.
