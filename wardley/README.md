# Wardley — Simon Wardley Intellectual Biography KB

Knowledge base for an intellectual biography of **Simon Wardley**, strategist, researcher, and creator of Wardley Mapping — a visual method for understanding competitive landscapes through value chains and component evolution.

Wardley's framework integrates military strategic thinking (Sun Tzu, Boyd), evolutionary economics, and systems thinking into a practical methodology for technology strategy, organizational design, and public policy. His work has influenced UK government digital transformation (GDS), technology strategy, and a global community of practitioners.

## Key Intellectual Themes

- **Situational awareness** — Strategy requires understanding position and movement before deciding what to do
- **Evolution** — All components evolve through predictable stages from genesis to commodity
- **The Strategy Cycle** — Purpose, Landscape, Climate, Doctrine, Leadership (Sun Tzu + Boyd)
- **Doctrine vs. gameplay** — Universal principles (doctrine) vs. context-dependent choices (gameplay)
- **Inertia** — Past success creates resistance to evolutionary change
- **Pioneer-Settler-Town Planner** — Different evolutionary stages require different organizational approaches
- **Open knowledge** — CC BY-SA licensing; strategy knowledge as commons, not commodity
