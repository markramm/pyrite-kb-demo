Daniel T. Jones (b. 1946), British researcher and [[james-womack]]'s co-author on the foundational lean texts. Jones brought a European manufacturing perspective to the MIT IMVP study and co-authored [[the-machine-that-changed-the-world]] and [[lean-thinking]]. He founded the Lean Enterprise Academy (UK counterpart to Womack's [[lean-enterprise-institute]]) and has been particularly influential in extending lean thinking beyond manufacturing into services, healthcare, and government. Jones's work emphasizes the [[value-stream]] perspective — understanding the complete flow of value from customer need to customer satisfaction.
