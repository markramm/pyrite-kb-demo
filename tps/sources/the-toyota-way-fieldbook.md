The practical companion to [[the-toyota-way]], providing detailed guidance on implementing [[jeffrey-liker|Liker]]'s 14 principles. Published by McGraw-Hill, 2005. Written with David Meier, a former Toyota group leader with hands-on TPS experience, the Fieldbook bridges the gap between understanding TPS conceptually and implementing it operationally. Includes exercises, self-assessments, and case studies. The book is particularly valuable for its coverage of the people-development dimension of TPS — how Toyota develops problem-solving capability through [[a3-thinking]], [[gemba]] mentoring, and structured on-the-job development.
