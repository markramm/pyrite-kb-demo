A concise introductory handbook to TPS concepts, created by Art Smalley and published by Art of Lean, Inc. (circa 2000). Intended as a simple guide introducing key TPS concepts — not a complete reference or implementation guide.

Covers the two pillars ([[just-in-time]] and [[jidoka]]), the elimination of waste ([[seven-wastes-muda]]), [[takt-time]], flow production, [[pull-production]] via [[kanban]], and [[heijunka]] (leveling). Useful as a quick reference and teaching tool.
