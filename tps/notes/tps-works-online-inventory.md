## 5. Ohno Interviews and Primary Sources

### 1984 Ohno Oral History (Shimokawa/Fujimoto, 3 hours)
- **Japanese transcript (U. Tokyo CIRJE):** https://www.cirje.e.u-tokyo.ac.jp/research/dp/97/j4/dp.pdf (scanned, image-only PDF — not OCR'd)
- **English excerpts (Gemba Academy):** https://blog.gembaacademy.com/2010/02/16/excerpts_from_an_interview_with_taiichi_ohno_july_1/
- **Video footage clips (Gemba Academy):** https://blog.gembaacademy.com/2017/03/13/taiichi-ohno-interview-video-footage/
- **Academic reference:** https://ideas.repec.org/p/tky/jseres/97j04.html
- **Key quotes from English excerpts:**
  - On kanban: "The aim of kanban is to make troubles come to the surface and link them to kaizen activity."
  - On standard work: "If the kanbans do not change for one month you are salary thieves."
  - On waste: Ohno deliberately accepted lower machine utilization to optimize overall flow
- **Status:** Japanese transcript available (scanned); English excerpts available; full English translation only in "The Birth of Lean" (2009)

### Ohno Lecture Video (1984)
- **Referenced in:** 『トヨタ生産方式の原点』(JMAM, 2014) — includes DVD of Ohno lecture
- **Goldratt Japan seminar (2023):** Professor Fujimoto analyzed the 1984 Ohno lecture video in an online seminar
- **Status:** Available only as DVD supplement to book

## 7. Practitioner Speeches and Articles

### Teruyuki Minoura — "The Thinking Production System" (2003)
- **PDF (Art of Lean):** https://artoflean.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/The_Thinking_Production_System_DraftTE5.pdf
- **PDF (LeanCPI):** https://leancpi.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/The-thinking-production-system-Minoura.pdf
- **Context:** Speech at 2003 Automotive Parts System Solution Fair, Tokyo
- **Key argument:** The "T" in TPS stands for "Thinking" as well as "Toyota." Emphasizes hito-zukuri (making people) over monozukuri (making things)
- **Status:** Freely accessible PDF
