## Overview

HCI researcher and artist, now at Cornell, who studied with Agre and became one of the most important developers of the 'critical technical practice' framework. Sengers's work applies critical technical practice to affective computing, reflective design, and HCI more broadly.

Her Carnegie Mellon work and later Cornell research represent the most sustained continuation of Agre's methodological program.
