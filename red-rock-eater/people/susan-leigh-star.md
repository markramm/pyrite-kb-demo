## Overview

Sociologist and information scientist known for foundational work on boundary objects, infrastructure studies, and the sociology of science. Star co-edited (with Bowker, Turner, and Gasser) the volume in which Agre's 'Toward a Critical Technical Practice' was published. Her theoretical work on how categories and classifications shape experience has deep resonances with Agre's capture model.
