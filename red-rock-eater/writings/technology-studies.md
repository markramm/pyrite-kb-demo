CALL FOR VIEWPOINT CONTRIBUTIONS FOR TS

TECHNOLOGY STUDIES (TS) is a new multidisciplinary journal published
by Walter de Gruyter#Berlin#New York.  As indicated by its title, the
journal is a central vehicle for international scholarly debate about
`technology'.

One regular feature in TS is the Viewpoint section, which provides
insights and information otherwise rarely shared.  Forthcoming issues
might feature anecdotes or narratives about how a particular
innovation came about.  This section is intended to be less formal
than an article, commentary or research note and may be more
conversational than what is usually read in an academic journal.

TS is soliciting contributions of this type for publication in
upcoming issues and would appreciate your participation.  If you have
something you think is suitable and would like to contribute, we
would be delighted to hear from you.  Please send us a brief
description of what you would like to contribute and how you plan to
develop the piece.  Explain why you think this might be of interest
to TS and its readers.  Include a short bio of yourself, with
complete mailing address, phone, fax and E-mail numbers.

For examples of previous Viewpoint publications, see the first two
issues of the TS journal, or send a request to the TS Editorial
Office by E-mail to:  GATTIKER2@CETUS.MNGT.ULETH.CA

or write to:

 Professor Urs Gattiker, Editor
 Centre for Technology Studies
 Faculty of Management
 The University of Lethbridge
 Lethbridge, AB  T1K 3M4
 CANADA                          Fax: 403 329-2038

Thank you for your consideration; we look forward to receiving some
interesting pieces for possible publication.
