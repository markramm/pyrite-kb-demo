[KCRW is a cool public radio station in Santa Monica.  Not only do
they carry most of the National Public Radio shows, but they produce
numerous shows on their own.  They have three channels of streaming

<http://www.kcrw.org/>.  And if you do listen to them over the net,

Q: In mathematics, what is a "perfect" number?

A: A perfect number is one whose proper divisors sum to the number
itself (e.g., the number 6 has proper divisors 1, 2 and 3...and 1 + 2- 3 = 6.  The number 28 has divisors 1, 2, 4, 7 and 14...and 1 + 2 + 4- 7 + 14 = 28.
