Dear Phil
Thanks for your support. Please feel free to send my message to as many 
people as possible.
Here is the latest:

Dear friends and comrades

OUR PRINTING PRESS HAS BEEN BOMBED

As I said yesterday, several youths burned several thousand copies OF THE 
STATE-OWNED Herald in retaliation for the  burning of the Daily News copies 
by war vets.
This is the statement released by Ray Mungoshi, Editor of the Herald:
"This is the last thing I expected to happen  in a society like ours where 
the law of the jungle was outlawed at independence. People should be 
tolerant to opposing views and allow those who hold opinions at variance 
with theirs to express themselves freely.
"If the MDC was indeed behind this attack then its leadership should be held 
accountable for the actions of its members.
"in any democracy people should not fear to speak their minds even in the 
presence of those they know who do not subscribe to their point of view. 
Freedom of expression is one of the bed rocks of democracy."

and Jonathan Moyo, Minister of Information:
"We think this is an unprecedented act of thuggery and destruction of public 
property. This serves to confirm the assertion by the public that  the Daily 
News is a publication of the MDC. It is now clear that the MDC runs the 
Daily News."

This morning, the printing press of the Daily News was extensively bombed, 
rendering the press useless. The government has not yet commented.

Anyway, the above two gentlemen have yet to comment on the bombing of Daily 
News offices, the attempted assassination of the editor-in-chief, the 
bombing of MDC offices, last year, and this year,  the burning of Daily News 
papers, the attacks on the deputy news editor, Julius Zava, and the breaking 
of our office windows (twice this week), all by war vets, non of whom have 
been arrested..
Please send this message to any journalist friends and human rights groups 
all over the world. We need your moral support. I would like to add, 
however, that moral at our office is at its highest, and we can't wait to 
produce tomorrow's paper.
We shall overcome!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

editorial@dailynews.co.zw

Julius Zava is on:
dailynews7@dailynews.co.zw
