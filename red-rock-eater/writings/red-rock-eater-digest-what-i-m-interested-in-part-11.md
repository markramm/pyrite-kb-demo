but I was wrong.  This list consists mostly of recent books that I
will read someday plus a batch of older books that I've more or less
read and recommend.  The rules, as usual, are that books are on this
list for many reasons, and that you should draw no conclusions from
the presence or absence of any of them.  I hope the list is useful.
