Job Opening
Communication, Culture & Technology Program (CCT)
Georgetown University
Washington, DC

Director, CCT Networked Media Center

The CCT Networked Media Center is an innovative new training and
development facility sponsored by the graduate program in Communication,
Culture and Technology at Georgetown University. The Media Center 
provides an exciting opportunity to take a leadership role in information
technology and new media at Georgetown and in the Washington, DC area, 
and will combine advanced educational applications and business
entrepreneurship.

The Media Center Director will manage the technical, educational, and
business operations of the CCT Media Center, which will include an
administrative, technical, and teaching staff.

Candidates must understand the role of information technology in higher
education and must also have an entrepreneurial drive for forming
relationships with area business leaders. Knowledge of and experience with
digital media and the Internet/Web client-server environment is essential.
Candidates must have a BA or BS in a relevant field; advanced degree
preferred. Management and/or training experience required. Candidates
should familiarize themselves with the CCT Program philosophy and content
before applying.

For further information and application procedures, see the CCT Program
Website:
http://www.georgetown.edu/grad/CCT/

bishopk@gusun.georgetown.edu
