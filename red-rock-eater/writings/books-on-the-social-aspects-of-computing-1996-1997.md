  Copyright 1997 by the author.  Feel free to forward this bibliography

  Version of 4 January 1997. 

This is a bibliography of books on the social, cultural, political,
economic, and business aspects of networking and computing that were
published in English in 1996 and 1997.  It is probably incomplete.  It
is biased by my own interests and does not include primarily technical
books or directories of online resources.  All the same, if you know
about other books that belong on the list, by all means send me a full
citation and I'll include it in any subsequent edition.  Likewise, I'd
much appreciate hearing about any errors.
