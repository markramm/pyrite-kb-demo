# Mike Corrigan

```
Dear Friends of the Red Rock Eater,

Just a brief note to let you know that Mike Corrigan <mcorrigan@ucsd.edu>,
the systems person for the UCSD social sciences mainframe, will be leaving
UCSD.  He has unfortunately been laid off in a restructuring of the social
sciences computing facility here.  Mike has maintained the technical side
of RRE since its inception almost three years and a thousand messages ago.
Given that the world still does not have easy-to-use bullet-proof software
for maintaining large mailing lists, there is no way that RRE would have
happened without Mike's extensive voluntary assistance.  Can we please
give him a round of applause?

Thanks very much

Phil
```
