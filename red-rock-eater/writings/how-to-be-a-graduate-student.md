```
Greetings!

Back in the mists of time, I circulated a request for inputs
and suggestions on a paper I was writing that was intended as
a "handbook" of sorts for graduate students and advisors.  I've
finally finished a complete draft, and am making it publicly
available for anyone who's interested.  At some point I will
submit it for publication in a journal (maybe CACM or Science
if they're interested), but right now I'm just interested in
distributing it as widely as possible to anybody who might find
it useful, and getting comments and feedback on the paper.

The paper is available by ftp at ftp.erg.sri.com.  There is
a latex file (advice.tex), with two additional input files
(advice.bbl, the BibTeX bibliography, and named.sty, a bibliography
style file), and a postscript version (advice.ps).  To get the
paper:
    ftp to ftp.erg.sri.com, login as anonymous, and give your
    	e-mail address as the password
    'cd pub/advice'
    use the 'get' command to take whichever files you want.
To generate the latex output, copy the first three files, run
'latex advice,' then 'bibtex advice,' then latex twice more
to incorporate all of the references.

Some of the references are incomplete (or possibly incorrect).
If anyone has more complete bibligraphic information for any of
the references, I'd appreciate it if you would send it to me.
Comments and feedback on the paper are also very welcome.

I'm about to start maternity leave (today is my last day at work),
so I may be slow to respond to e-mail, but I will be logging on
periodically, and I promise I will get back to you eventually!

Marie desJardins
marie@erg.sri.com
```
