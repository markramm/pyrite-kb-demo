Greetings,

I don't agree with all the conclusions, but listed below are some
very interesting and important resources, dealing with the
intersection of ethnicity, culture, class, poverty, computers, and
cyberspace. 

Even if you're busy, please be sure to at least browse them. 

If you have a web page, and you agree that these links are
important, please do me a favor and add them to a section on your
site. Thank you. 

By the way, most of the links lead to original material, not the 
summarized articles you may have read in a newspaper or magazine
dealing with similar subjects.
