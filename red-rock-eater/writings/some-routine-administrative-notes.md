# some routine administrative notes

```
RRE is still in its summertime low gear.

As a periodic reminder, you can stop your subscription to RRE by sending a
message that looks like this:

  To: rre-request@weber.ucsd.edu
  Subject: unsubscribe

Make sure you "unsubscribe" from the same account you used to "subscribe".

If your mailer tells me "User unknown" then I'll stop your subscription myself
to avoid getting overwhelmed with bouncemail.  You're welcome to start your
subscription up again, though.  Just send a message that looks like:

  To: rre-request@weber.ucsd.edu
  Subject: subscribe firstname lastname

Whether you're a longtimer or a newcomer, you might wish to have a look at 
the RRE Archive, which currently contains over 150 really interesting files.
To get an annotated index to the Archive, send a message that looks like:

  To: rre-request@weber.ucsd.edu
  Subject: archive send index

Feel free to pass this index along to anyone else on the net who might be
interested.  It includes the instructions needed to retrieve the files and
learn more about RRE.

Finally, you'd be most welcome to send me anything you think might be worth
passing along to the 1600 RRE subscribers worldwide.  I may not pass it along,
but I'll certainly read it and acknowledge it.  Even if I don't send it to the
whole list, I can usually send it to specific people who might like to know
about it.

Thanks a lot.

Phil
```
