Dear patient participants in this effort, please send this announcement
to all appropriate lists, sites, and people. Subscription to Inet-news
is now open.

ANNOUNCING A LIST FOR PEOPLE WHO NEED NEWS

INET-NEWS is a low volume listserv which features :

1    announcements of new sources for NEWS

2    analysis of existing NEWS sources

3    requests to develop teams of NEWS monitors and discussants

4    descriptions of specialized collections of NEWS sources

5    discussion of how to use NEWS on the net

The list is designed to make internet NEWS resources better known.
And to encourage public use of, and interaction with the NEWS.

Coverage includes NEWS :

A     delivered by email, usenet, listservs, gophers and WWW.

B     provided daily, weekly, monthly and irregularly

C     available free, for a fee, or both

D     produced by volunteers, papers, broadcasters, and businesses

E     covering local regional national and international events

F     including commercial news, public relations, and propaganda

G     in any and all languages and covering all cultures

The list will be useful to the public and :

Teachers (languages, geography, current events, media)

Journalists and reporters

Activists and non profit groups

Internet service providers, civic networks and free-nets

Public relations firms, and other seeking coverage

---

subscriptions are as follows:

Send email to:  listserv@nstn.ca
Subject: <none>
Message Body:

          subscribe INET-NEWS <your full name>

For more information, contact Sam Sternberg <aa002@Torfree.net>
