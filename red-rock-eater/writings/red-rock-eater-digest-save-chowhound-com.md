Dear RRE Subscribers,

Chowhound is an extremely virtuous Web site where food enthusiasts
compare notes on local restaurants.  If you want recommendations for
good Thai food in West LA, just post a query on chowhound.com and you
will get several reliable recommendations.  Chowhound has been run on
a mostly volunteer basis, but the volunteers are about to go bankrupt.
Its death would be a tragedy, and so I hope you will help save it.
Just go to chowhound.com, click past the introductory text, browse
the discussions if you haven't, and read the page about the Chowmarket.
You can buy stuff to support their work, or you can simply give them
money.  Act now, before it's too late.

Thanks a lot

Phil
