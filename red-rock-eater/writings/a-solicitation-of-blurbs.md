# a solicitation of blurbs

```
The Red Rock Eater News Service will soon celebrate its second anniversary.
To mark this occasion, I would like to collect some endorsements of RRE from
its satisfied subscribers that I can use in advertising the list to others.
In the United States anyway, these endorsements are called "blurbs", such as
the brief statements from prominent authors that one encounters on the back
covers of books.  Please include your name and whatever affiliation you would
like to be identified by.  For example,

  Professor J. H. MacDonald
  Department of Veterinary Dentistry
  University of Perth
  Perth, Australia

  Abigail Mirsky
  Field Organizer
  Mothers Against Neoclassical Economics
  Chicago, Illinois

  Jim Carter
  Second Grade Teacher
  Thaddeus Hoagland Elementary School
  Athens, Georgia

My experience is that people on the Internet want mailing lists to be useful
and to have a high ratio of useful stuff to nonsense.  If your own blurb could
explain concisely and intriguingly how RRE has been useful to you then that
would be just great.  Other categories of praise for RRE are, of course, most
welcome.

Thanks very much.

Phil Agre
```
