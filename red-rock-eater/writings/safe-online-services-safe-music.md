The purpose of this list is to provide a forum for discussion by online
users and online system administrators on how to bring decency, integrity,
affordability, and safety into being online. People are welcome to discuss
their feelings on what changes they would like to see in the online
industry, how to create a family safe online environment, how to make
people aware of the family safe online resources available, and other
topics centered around the development, creation, and maintenance of
family safe online services. Advertising is acceptable as long as the
frequency of advertisement is not more than one in a seven day period, and
the advertisement must be for family safe sites. In addition, the
advertisments can not contain ANY advertisements for adult related
materials. The maintainers of this list reserve the right to make other
restrictions on ads as they feel necessary.

Finally, this resource is provided free of charge by the International
Community Online Network (ICON) who is dedicated to providing family safe
online Bulletin Board Services (BBSs) and Web sites. Please look for the
ICON logo on all the sites you visit, and encourage others to join our
cause!  To learn more about ICON visit our web site at
http://www.atlsky.com/icon/icon.htm
