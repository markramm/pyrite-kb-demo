Conservatives Chop at Salon's Family Tree
After breaking stories about
Richard Mellon Scaife funneling money to
The American Spectator, Salon falls under
attack from conservatives =97 and is forced
to fend off charges of close ties with Democrats.
OJR's Matt Welch delves into the war of words.
<http://www.ojr.org/pr/news_salon_partisan.htm>

---

NetMedia '98: Media and the Internet
Keynote speakers at the London conference offered
a variety of perspectives on online journalism =97
and pointers for success on the Web.
OJR Guest Contributor J.L. Perrone reports.
<http://www.ojr.org/pr/news_netmedia1.htm>

Mitnick on Trial: Trial Date Set
While a January trial date is a minor victory
for Kevin David Mitnick, his defense is still
being denied access to evidence, reports OJR hacker
expert Doug Thomas.
<http://www.ojr.org/pr/mitnick4.htm>

Another Brash Web Columnist

To Protect and Serve!
