# Arts & Technology Interactive Web Site

```
Date: Mon, 29 Jan 1996 18:12:50 GMT
From: kanter@tmn.com
Subject: Arts & Technology Interactive Web Site

January 29, 1996

ARTS AND TECHNOLOGY CONFERENCE HOSTS INTERACTIVE WEB SITE
AT http://thecity.sfsu.edu/users/GovConf/

The California Arts Council and the California Assembly of Local Arts
Agencies are sponsors of the "Governor's Conference on the Arts; The Role
of the Artist in the Digital Age," February 1-3, at the Hotel
Intercontinental, Los Angeles.
As part of the conference, the World Wide Web site will report on
activities, providing interviews, images, sounds, and articles by
award-winning digital culture/theater/film critic Scott Rosenberg.
Visitors to the site will be invited to provide commentary and feedback on
issues raised by the conference, as part of what will become a permanent
dialogue on the arts and technology on the Web.  Commentary will be a
critical part of developing arts and technology policies for the state and
local arts agencies.

"The Role of the Artist in the Digital Age" conference will explore
numerous topics regarding the role of internet and new media technologies
and art-making.  Leaders in non-profit arts organizations, business and
professional media makers and producers will speak, a number of special
installations and presentations are planned as well.

The conference Web Site currently lists information regarding the
conference schedule, speakers, and other links.

We invite you to join the conference from wherever you are.

The site is administered by the San Francisco Digital Media Center.  For
more information, please contact sfdmc@well.com, or call 415 824 9394.
```
