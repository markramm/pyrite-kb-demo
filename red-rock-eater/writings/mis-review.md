     Announcing a New Annual Publication by the MIS Quarterly

     MIS Review

     MIS Review is a new annual publication of the MIS Quarterly. Its
     mission is to promote MIS research by publishing articles that
     conceptualize research areas and survey and synthesize prior research.
     These articles will provide important input in setting directions for
     future research. MIS Review is designed as a readily available, widely
     recognized repository of the accumulated knowledge and research
     progress in the field of information systems.

     MIS Review will focus on original, high-quality review articles by
     accomplished scholars.  Both commissioned and submitted articles will
     be published. Leading scholars may be invited to write articles on
     their area of specialty; any scholar may submit an article for
     publication consideration. Scholars who wish to submit articles may
     obtain advice on suitability by sending the Senior Editor for MIS
     Review an abstract and outline of the proposed manuscript.

     Published articles will be broad in scope relative to a topic area and
     embrace multiple studies. They will be selected for their contribution
     to the development of MIS as an academic discipline by synthesizing
     prior research and providing a conceptual foundation for future
     research. The survey of research on a topic should include the major
     relevant work by scholars who are part of the international community
     of researchers. Articles fitting these criteria are expected to be in
     the range of 30 to 50 pages, including bibliography, when published.

     The review process is designed to enhance the quality of published
     articles and aid in identification of important contributions to the
     topic area. All articles will be reviewed by two or three senior
     scholars familiar with the topic of the article.  The quality of MIS
     Review will be consistent with the high quality of the MIS Quarterly.

     Further information on submission dates, publication schedule, and
     subscriptions will appear in the next issue of the MIS Quarterly.  In
     general, MIS Review will follow the manuscript guidelines for the MIS
     Quarterly.  The Senior Editor of MIS Review, Richard T. Watson
     (RWATSON@BLAZE.CBA.UGA.EDU) of the University of Georgia, is also a
     Senior Editor of the MIS Quarterly.

     Gordon B. Davis, Executive Editor
