# NEW: UNITED - Labor Union Discussions

```
Date: Wed, 13 Apr 1994 11:18:09 CDT
From: Len Wilson <lbw@netcom.com>
To: Multiple recipients of list NEW-LIST <NEW-LIST@VM1.NoDak.EDU>
Subject: NEW: UNITED - Labor Union Discussions
 
UNITED via UNITED-request@cougar.com
 
   UNITED is an unmoderated, uncensored e-mail discussion group intended
   for anyone with an interest in the labor movement. Subscribers may
   include members of any union, scholars or those interested in organizing
   a union. Discussions in UNITED will not be limited to events in the U.S.
 
   Especially welcome will be postings related to the following:

* Reports of labor struggles around the world* The decline of unionism in the U.S.* Bringing unions into the Information Age* Modern union communications* The role of unions in local, state and national politics

UNITED is an e-mail discussion group hosted by The Colorado Cougar BBS.
 
   Access to UNITED is available either by subscribing via e-mail over the
   Internet or by calling The Colorado Cougar BBS in Thornton, Colorado.
   On the BBS, it is in the "Solidarity" section where callers may access
   it through a message base like an echo or newsgroup.
 
   E-mail Access
   -------------
     Mail to:  UNITED-Info@cougar.com to receive this file.
 
     Mail to:  UNITED-Request@cougar.com with "SUBSCRIBE" in the
               message text to join or "UNSUBSCRIBE" to drop out.
 
     Mail to:  UNITED@cougar.com to submit a posting to UNITED.
 
     Mail to:  UNITED-Subs@cougar.com to get a list of subscribers.
 
   BBS Access
   ----------
     To access the BBS, call the number listed below (Denver area) and
     leave a message to the sysop requesting access to the union area
     of the board. First-time callers must give name, address, phone,
     etc.
 
     The Colorado Cougar BBS    data phone: 303-288-6009
     FidoNet  - 1:104/249       300 baud to 14.4 kilo-baud
     Internet - cougar.com
     Sysop - Len Wilson, IAM Local Lodge 1886
 
   Owner:  Len Wilson  Len.Wilson@cougar.com
 
   There are other lists on labor each with a different emphasis or a
   different style.  The following differentiate UNITED from them:
 
      1) It unmoderated and uncensored. This alone makes it unique.
      2) It doesn't focus on any particular field of study such as
         Law or History.
      3) It is primarily intended for members and officials of unions
         but scholars and others with an interest are WELCOME.
 
   The Colorado Cougar BBS is a member of Solinet, a network of
   labor-oriented BBS's around the country.  We have an echo (message
   base) named Laborchat which is gated to UNITED.
```
