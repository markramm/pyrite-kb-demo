Digital Diploma Mills?
A Second Look at Information Technology and Higher Education

April 24-April 25, 1998

This two-day conference will be the first major North American
conference devoted exclusively to a critical assessment of the use of
computer-based instructional technologies in higher education. Panels
will focus upon the political economy of the academic high-tech agenda,
the actual cost effectiveness and demonstrated pedagogical value of
instructional technologies, the consequences of their widespread use
for both students and faculty, and the implications for the future of
quality higher education.
