             COMMUNITY STATION SILENCED IN BARCELONA

                                       Barcelona, February 1996
Dear friends,

      for   the  past  five  years,  CONTRABANDA  FM  has  been
broadcasting  24  hours a day on 91.0 FM in Barcelona,  at  500
watts.  Suddenly, on January 19th, a new station  belonging  to
the  Barcelona  City Council, and which has no legally-assigned
frequency,  began to broadcast on our frequency,  91.0  FM,  at
5000 watts. Our signal has been completely drowned out.

CONTRABANDA FM is a free radio not controlled by any  political
or  religious  organization,  nor by  any  commercial  interest
whatsoever.  CONTRABANDA FM is the only  station  in  Barcelona
whose  studio  doors  are open to ecological,  anti-militarist,
women's,  immigrants', gay, solidarity and other  groups.  Many
people  have  used our microphones to express  their  opinions,
information, anxieties and concerns, in person, by  letter  and
by telephone-calls.

CONTRABANDA  FM is the only station covering the whole  of  the
Greater  Barcelona  area  with three million  inhabitants  that
provides  an  alternative to the commercial  and  institutional
broadcasters.

There  is  an  enormous contrast between  the  atitude  of  the
Barcelona  City Council in promoting an open, modern city,  the
capital  of  the  Mediterranean, and the fact  that  they  have
chosen  our  frequency,  91.0 FM,  thus  eliminating  the  only
genuinely open, modern voice on the dial; when the City Council
already  has  a  municipal radio for each  of  the  City's  ten
districts.

Therefore,  we ask you to support us by sending a letter  or  a
fax  to  the  Mayor  of  Barcelona at  some  of  the  following

          broadcasting  option  differing  from  those  belonging  to
          commercial interests or the administration.

      Thank you for your support!

      To: The Mayor of Barcelona, Mr. Pasqual Maragall

      Dear Sir,

           We  have  been informed that your administration has  been
      occupying  CONTRABANDA  FM's frequency,  91.0  FM,  since  19th
      January 1996.

            This  action  of  yours  silencing  CONTRABANDA  FM   has
      astounded us, as we value this radio's open, supportive role in
      giving  a voice to immigrant and other social groups which  are
      otherwise poorly represented.

           We  wish  to  state our disappointment with the  Barcelona
      City  Council for not respecting CONTRABANDA FM's existence,  a
      station which is known worlwide.

           We  ask  you,  with  all  due respect,  to  recognise  the
      existence   of  CONTRABANDA  FM  as  a  means  of  comunication
      essential  for pluralism in the Metropolitan Area. We therefore
      request  you  return  91.0  FM to  this  station  in  order  to
      guarantee its continuity.

           Firmly  believing that you will do all in  your  power  to
      ensure  the conditions necessary for CONTRABANDA FM to function
      at  91.0  FM,  we look forward to your reply at  your  earliest
      convenience.

          Yours sincerely,
