UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA, SAN DIEGO
DEPARTMENT OF COMMUNICATION 
ASSISTANT PROFESSOR, TENURE-TRACK

The University of California, San Diego, Department of Communication is
seeking to fill a position beginning Fall, 1997, in the area of political
economy of communication.  It would be a tenure-track position at the
assistant professor level.  We are looking for someone with expertise in
one or more of the following fields of research: mass media industries,
policies and law; the organization and regulation of telecommunications
and new technologies; international communications and the role of
communications in developing countries.

Salaries are in strict accordance with UC pay scales.  Send vita, statement
of research and teaching interests, and names of 3 references by October
15, 1996, or until the position is filled to:

		Recruitment (POST)
		Department of Communication (0503)
		UCSD
		9500 Gilman Drive
		La Jolla, CA  92093-0503

If non-citizen, state immigration status.  EOE/AAE.
