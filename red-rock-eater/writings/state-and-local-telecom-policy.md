                        A Boulder Communications Forum:

             RE-THINKING STATE AND LOCAL TELECOMMUNICATIONS POLICY

                        December 11th and 12th, 1995

A conference on Re-Thinking State and Local Telecommunications Policy
will be held on Monday, December 11, 1995 through Tuesday, December 12,
1995 at the University of Colorado at Boulder.

At a time when the U.S. Congress is trying to pass sweeping
telecommunications legisla-tion, state and local governments are facing
critical issues in a rapidly changing technological and industrial
environment.  At this conference, the Communications Initiative of the
University of Colorado at Boulder will bring together leading national
and regional figures from the public and private sectors to discuss the
stakes for industry and the general public in the changes taking place in
telecommunications policy at the state and local levels.

Who Should Attend?  This conference will be of interest to
telecommunications policy researchers and analysts, policy makers at both
state and municipal levels, and representatives of the cable,
broadcasting, telephone, and public utility industries.
