# Municipal Telecommunications List

```
Date:    Sat, 19 Nov 1994 11:17:24 +0001
From:    Miles R Fidelman <fidelman@CIVICNET.ORG>
Subject: Municipal Telecommunications List

Hi Folks,

Given the recent spate of discussion of local government issues, I
thought it might be appropriate to repost this:

Municipal Telecommunications Mailing List

---

This list focuses on the design, use, and policy of municipal
telecommunications networks, including regional networks supporting
multiple municipalities (e.g. regional library networks).

Currently, this list is open to all subscribers, but our intent is that
this be a forum for municipal officials and staff, such as MIS directors,
telecom directors, cable commissioners, GIS system managers, library
automation managers, dispatch system operators, etc.; and secondarily a
forum for those who provide equipment and services to the municipal
telecom environment.

The intent is to focus on nitty gritty design issues surrounding the
construction of municipal networks, including:

- city/town-wide data networking
- use of cable television I-net infrastructure to support town needs
- consolidation of school/library/municipal networks
- regional network approaches for rural areas
- purchasing and negotiation strategies

This list is not intended for the discussion of community networks other
than as they are supported by and interact with municipal networks - i.e.
this is not COMMUNET.

This list is not intended for the discussion of regulatory issues, other
than as they impact municipal acquisition of networking facilities and
services - i.e. this is not telecomreg.

Subscription requests, info requests, and unsubscribe requests
should be sent to the automated list manager at majordomo@civicnet.org -
NOT to the list itself (muni-telecom). The following commands, typed
EXACTLY as shown (i.e. don't put you name or address after a command),
will be recognized by majordomo:

help
info muni-telecom
subscribe muni-telecom
unsubscribe muni-telecom

help will send back more info on majordomo
info will send back info on the list
subscribe will add you to the list
unsubscribe will remove you

If you have a signature at the end of your message, put a single line
containing the word    end   after your commands to majordomo - otherwise
majordomo will try to act on your signature and send back errors.

To send a message to the list, send it to muni-telecom@civicnet.org

To reach the (human) list owner, send to muni-telecom-owner@civicnet.org
```
