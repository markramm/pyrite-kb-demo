POSITION DESCRIPTION

INTERNET ACTIVIST
Electronic Privacy Information Center
Washington, DC

The Electronic Privacy Information Center is an Internet civil
liberties organization, based in Washington, DC. EPIC pursues
litigation, conducts public education, testifies in Congress,
organizes conferences, coordinates grassroots advocacy,
publishes books, reports, and a newsletter. EPIC maintains
several well regarded web sites.

Description: Maintain and manage office computers, LAN,
peripheral and other equipment; maintain several web sites and
mailing lists; monitor newsgroups, electronic mailing lists and
domestic and foreign news sites for stories of interest relating
to privacy and free speech; manage EPIC Online Bookstore; assist
EPIC staff on special projects as required.

Qualifications: Strong interest in and commitment to civil
liberties issues relating to the Internet, particularly free
speech, privacy, and encryption; knowledgeable in
Ethernet/Appletalk LAN, web design, HTML, web development,
mailservers, listservers, Mac OS, Linux, and MS Word; good
writing and communications skills; well organized and reliable.

Salary: $25,000-$35,000 plus benefits

Application Deadline: May 1, 1999

Budget: < $500,000

Web: http://www.epic.org

Contact:  Marc Rotenberg, executive director, Electronic Privacy
Information Center, 666 Pennsylvania Ave., SE Suite 301
Washington, DC 20002 +1 202 544 9240 (tel) +1 202 547 5482 (fax)

e-mail:jobs@epic.org

EPIC is an equal opportunity employers. Women and members of
minority groups are encouraged to apply.
