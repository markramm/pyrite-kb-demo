Introduction to the conference

---

The conference is organised by the Centre for Social History 
and the Humanities Research Centre

The conference will discuss:

--- the history and poetics of the letter
--- the letter as historical artifact and literary form
--- the letter as historical evidence

From 1600 to the Present day, focusing on the topics of:

letters home      letters from the front         business letters
begging letters      the epistolary form        women's letter-writing
mass literacy and letter writing       model letters          
letters and masculinity
