# Pentium 2001 parody was copyrighted

```
I've been informed that the 2001 parody making fun of the Pentium chip is
actually copyrighted, having originally appeared in the 12/13/94 issue of
Computer Currents.  The author was Lincoln Spector and the original title
was "2001.267".  Needless to say, the correct attribution and copyright
notice did not survive in the version that I received and forwarded to RRE.
Nonetheless, I apologize to Mr. Spector and Computer Currents and encourage
everyone not to forward the message any further.

Phil Agre
```
