## Information Studies Seminar UCLA Department of Information Studies 2002-2003 School Year

The UCLA Information Studies Seminar is a seminar series, held on occasional
Thursdays from 3pm to 5pm, that draws speakers from every discipline relating
to information and its place in society. The seminars are held in the
GSE&IS Building (just west of the Research Library), room 111. All events
are free and everyone is welcome to attend.

Further events and details will be posted as they become definite.

Parking information. For those who are driving to UCLA, the best parking
structure is lot 5, at the Royce entrance to UCLA along Sunset Blvd (along
the north edge of campus). Parking permits ($6) are available from the
kiosks along Hilgard Ave (along the east edge of campus), or at the entrance
to lot 4, at the Westwood Blvd entrance along Sunset Blvd. You can also get
a campus map from the kiosk. Note that the parking permits often don't work
on the top level of lot 5. If you can't get a permit for lot 5, the second
best choice is lot 4.
