# please help my brilliant students

```
Dear Friends,

The brilliant students in my "Communication and Computers" class are
writing papers that analyze how certain genres of communication, both
on and off the Internet, fit into the lives of the people who use them.
Examples of these genres include political magazines, rave posters,
company home pages, real estate ads, graphic novels, Philipino movies,
driver education films, action alerts, anarchist propaganda, how-to
books, and coupons in the Sunday newspaper.

Drafts of these papers are now available on the Web at the following URL:

  http://communication.ucsd.edu/pagre/internet-papers.html

Would you be willing to offer comments and suggestions on one of these
papers?  In doing so you'll learn something interesting, you'll help
one of my brilliant students, and you'll contribute to a revolution
in undergraduate teaching and research.  We would be very grateful if
you would consider helping out.

Thanks very much.

Phil Agre
```
