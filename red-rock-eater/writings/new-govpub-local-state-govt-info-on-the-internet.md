# NEW: GOVPUB - Local & State Govt. Info on the Internet

```
Date:    Thu, 9 Nov 1995 15:18:43 CST
From:    Dana Noonan <NOONAN@MSUS1.MSUS.EDU>
Subject: NEW: GOVPUB - Local & State Govt. Info on the Internet

GOVPUB on LISTSERV@VM1.NODAK.EDU     Local & State Govt. Info on Internet

   GOVPUB is a discussion list devoted to the practical and
   policy issues related to providing local and state government
   information on the Internet. It is not a forum for political
   debate.

   This list is intended for agency professional and technical staff
   involved in making public information available on online, but
   anyone with an interest in the topic is welcome to join the list.

   If you would like to join the GOVPUB discussion list, send e-mail to
   LISTSERV@VM1.NODAK.EDU  and include

      SUBSCRIBE GOVPUB yourfirstname yourlastname

   in the body of your e-mail letter, replacing yourfirstname
   and yourlastname with your first and last name.

   Owner:  Dana Noonan  noonan@msus1.msus.edu
```
