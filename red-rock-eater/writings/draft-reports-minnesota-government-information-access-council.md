Over the last year the Council has been developing principles and
recommendations within four working groups. The working groups have
essentially finished drafting their principles and recommendations.

includes two individuals from each working group.

The composition team will send drafts back to the working groups and
to the Executive Committee to ensure that the spirit of each group's
work is covered in the final report. The final report will be
presented to the legislature when the session begins in mid-January, 1996.

North Star Demonstration Project

A main focus of GIAC over the summer was the August launch of the
North Star Demonstration Project from Governor Carlson's office. North
Star is the name of the citizen-focused starting point on the World-Wide-Web
for government information in Minnesota. GIAC staff, with the Infomation
Policy Office and Department of Administration, coordinate the project.
North Star has received very positive comments from the public and government
staff as well as media attention. North Star and the work of many state
agencies and government organizations at all level are helping to illustrate
the potential of electronic access to government information and services.
For those with WWW access, North Star is located at: http://www.state.mn.us

North Star Announcements

Would you like to receive periodic updates on North Star and public
