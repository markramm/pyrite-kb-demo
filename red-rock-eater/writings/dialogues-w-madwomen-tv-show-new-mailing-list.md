# Dialogues w/ Madwomen (TV show) - new mailing list

```
Date: 29 Jul 1994 13:11:04 -0400
From: povonline@aol.com (POV ONLINE)
Newsgroups: alt.abuse.recovery
Subject: Dialogues w/ Madwomen (TV show) - new mailing list
Organization: America Online, Inc. (1-800-827-6364)

Public TV's award-winning nonfiction film series P.O.V. has set up a
mailing list for questions, comments and discussion about DIALOGUES WITH
MADWOMEN, a fascinating introduction to the world of madness from seven
women who've been there and come back to tell the tale.  Many PBS stations
will air the show August 2, 1994, but call your public TV station for local
broadcast times.

To join the mailing list, send an email message to 

majordomo@igc.apc.org

with the message, "subscribe pov-madwomen" as the text (DON'T include
the ")

The seven women featured in DIALOGUES take viewers inside the world of
manic depression, multiple personality, schizophrenia, euphoria and
recovery.  Academy Award-winner Allie Light uses dream-like reenactments to
take us into these realms in her feature-length film, inspired by her own
experience with madness and co-produced with partner Irving Saraf.
Cassettes are distributed by Women Make Movies (462 Broadway, Suite 500,
New York, NY 10013, phone 212/925-0606 or fax 212/925-2052).

Comments and questions from this conference will be forwarded to producers
of DIALOGUES WITH MADWOMEN and P.O.V.
```
