Virtual Conference

http://www.cs.uchicago.edu/discussions/cpsr/annual/virtual.html

The aim of the "virtual conference" is to allow CPSR members and other
interested parties who cannot travel to Chicago for the meeting to
have a greater role in the conference itself, and to foster discussion
of the issues of the conference both before and after.

During the days of the conference, we will be updating the web pages
to reflect its proceedings. Position statements from speakers are
being made available before the conference to spark discussion.
Selected questions that arise in the discussion will be submitted to
the panels during the conference. Question and answer sessions will be
transcribed and made available soon after each session.

UPDATE: Panelists selected

http://www.cs.uchicago.edu/discussions/cpsr/annual/schedule.html

The on-line schedule (URL above) now contains the names of the
panelists for all five of the conference plenaries.

UPDATE: Position statements

Position statements from about half of the conference's panelists are
now available. They can be accessed from the schedule or from the
virtual conference pages.
