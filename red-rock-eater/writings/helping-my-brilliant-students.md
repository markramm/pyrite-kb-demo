# helping my brilliant students

```
Some of RRE's excellent subscribers have already responded to my appeal
for people to comment upon my brilliant students' term paper drafts.  
Here are a few more details.  Their papers are due on March 19th, so
any comments before then will be deeply appreciated.  If you can't help
out right now, why not mark that date in your calendar?  Some comments
on the next-to-final draft over the weekend of the 16th and 17th would
be especially useful.

To help out, just aim your web browser at our URL:

  http://communication.ucsd.edu/pagre/internet-papers.html

Then pick whatever paper title or topic interests you, and click.  The
class page includes clickable e-mail addresses for each author, so it's
easy to make comments by sending e-mail.  Most of the papers also have
e-mail addresses on them as well.  We're particularly hoping for comments
on the substance of the paper: how well does the paper analyze the way
that particular communicative materials fit into people's lives?

Thanks very much for your help.

Phil
```
