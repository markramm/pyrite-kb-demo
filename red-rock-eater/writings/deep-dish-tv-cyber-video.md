# Deep Dish TV & Cyber-Video

```
Date: Thu, 12 May 1994 09:25:49 -0400 (EDT)
From: CCTV (Channel 17) <cctv@emba.uvm.edu>
To: nii_agenda@civicnet.org
Subject: Deep Dish TV & Cyber-Video

Are you producing video information about the information age, 
cyberspace, civic nets, the technological millenium or related topics
that can be used to educate and train in local communities?

Have you been to an Internet or Electronic Highway Conference that has 
been recorded and we should know about?

Deep Dish TV and Paper Tiger TV are planning and producing a Cyber-series 
and want to know what's is out there. Please contact me with your 
references and resources so we can compile as comprehensive a list as 
possible of TV to learn by.

ALSO - If you live near a cable company with a public access TV channel, 
call them and ask for your DEEP DISH TV.

The new spring 1994 series: SICK AND TIRED OF BEING SICK AND TIRED (a 
radical, grassroots view of the health care crisis) premieres on
Thursday, May 19th from 1-2PM, and will be beamed down every Thursday 
through July 21st.

When you make the call to request the series ask them to point their 
satellite dish to

Satellite: Galaxy 1R (the replacement for Galaxy 1)
Transponder: 15
Horizontal: 4000
Longitude: 133 degrees West

Write or call for more information: the Deep Dish TV headquarters at 
212-473-8933.

Thanks for your interest and help in liberating the airwaves.
```
