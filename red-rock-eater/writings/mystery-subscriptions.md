# mystery subscriptions

```
Periodically, maybe every few weeks, somebody writes to claim that they 
have been receiving messages from RRE without ever having subscribed.  
These people seem to have nothing in common, and I have never heard 
of any of them.  I have begun to wonder if some weird person out there
is forging subscription messages.  It wouldn't be hard: just write a
demon that trawls newsgroups and listservs for addresses and signs them
up for random mailing lists.  Has anybody ever heard of such a thing?
It's hard to imagine what the thrill would be, given that it would be
difficult to observe the resulting minor havoc.

Phil
```
