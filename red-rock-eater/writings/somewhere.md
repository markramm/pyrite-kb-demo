## View your friends... Somewhere.

Somewhere's a place to keep up with what your friends are doing online.

We all have an online presence; made up of blogs, journals, social networks, photo sites, and more. And then there's online status, mood, music... the list goes on.

Somewhere takes an entirely new approach that makes it *easy* to see what's new with your friends—no matter where they hang out, how often they change their status, or whether they go Somewhere.

There's more. But we'll tell you about that later.

You and your friends will want to be Somewhere, soon.
