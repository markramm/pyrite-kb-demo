CALL FOR CONTRIBUTIONS

Dear Colleague

I am writing on behalf of the editors of the new international journal
Personal Technologies to invite you to provide a contribution.

Personal Technologies will provide a forum for the publication of research
in handheld and mobile computing and communications appliances.  The
journal will carry a wide range of contributions which represent the most
innovative and significant work in this new and developing area.

The journal is published by Springer-Verlag UK, who have an excellent
reputation for publishing international journals, conference proceedings
and research monographs.  The journal is due to launch in March 1997, the
first issue presenting a state-of-the-art overview of personal technologies
with contributions by Mike Hawley (MIT Media Lab), Steve Mann (MIT Media
Lab), Peter Cochrane (BT), Mark Weiser (Xerox), Joel Birnbaum
(Hewlett-Packard), and David Frohlich, Peter Thomas, Mike Hawley and
Kenkichi Hirade, Editors of Personal Technologies.

If you have any questions about a possible contribution, please do not
hesitate to contact me at Annette.Brune@csm.uwe.ac.uk or Peter Thomas at
Peter.Thomas@csm.uwe.ac.uk.  Further details are available at our WWW site
at URL

http://gate.uwe.ac.uk:8000/csm/research/PIM/PeTe.html
