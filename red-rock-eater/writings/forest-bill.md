# forest bill

```
Date: Sun, 9 Apr 1995 11:34:52 -0800
From: davidw@hevanet.com (David Wolfe)

[...]

   * * *  If you do nothing else today, ACT NOW to save our forests  * * *

Call on President Clinton to veto a bill recently passed by Congress which
includes an "emergency salvage" rider.  On March 15th, the House passed the
Taylor "Death to the forests" rider by 275 to 150.  This rider was attached
to the Supplemental & Recissions Act and forces the Forest Service to cut
down 6 billion board feet of timber, nearly twice as much as planned over
the next two years.  All other environmental laws will be overridden,
citizens will be powerless to stop the logging.  Other court settlements
such as Option 9 are void.  Last week the Senate passed HR1158 which
includes a variant, the Gorton Salvage Rider.  We have about 3 weeks until
congress reconvenes and forward this bill to President Clinton for
signature.  Urge the president@whitehouse.gov to veto this bill.

As a native Oregonian it is plainly obvious that our nation's forests are
being decimated, acre by acre, year by year.  The current "forest health
crisis" is a timber industry HOAX, an excuse for massive logging, claiming
that forests are sick and need to be "cured" by being cut down.

The Foothills Wildlife Recovery Project, the Forest Service's national
salvage logging model, involved massive violations of federal and state
environmental laws, over 2200 violations, resulting in destruction of
fisheries, soil, and wildlife habitat on 60,000 acres, with evidence of
rampant timber theft with routine collusion of the Forest Service.

What can we do now?  Urge President Clinton to uphold existing conservation
laws by vetoing the recently passed bill.  For more information call Save
America's Forests at (202) 544-9219.  Get the latest update at (202)
686-5888.

Write letters to the editor demanding that Congress stop stealing America's
forests and that your local paper do a story on this irrevocable situation.
```
