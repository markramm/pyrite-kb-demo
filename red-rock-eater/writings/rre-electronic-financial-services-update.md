Date: Thu, 15 Jul 1999 19:46:35 -0700
From: John Muller <johnmuller@earthlink.net>
Subject: Electronic Financial Services Update

Privacy developments once again lead off the monthly roundup of new
and previously-overlooked links in electronic financial services
and electronic commerce law, policy and protocols.  Previous updates
are available at the Web site of the American Bar Association Joint
Subcommittee on Electronic Financial Services,
http://www.abanet.org/buslaw/efss/whatsnew.html

A. FINANCIAL PRIVACY AND CONSUMER PROTECTION

H.R. 10 on financial modernization passes House of Representatives
(with Title V on financial privacy, Section 351 on confidentiality
of health and medical information, Section 171 on ATM fee disclosure
and Section 195 mandating a study on adapting existing banking laws
to online transactions)
<http://thomas.loc.gov/cgi-bin/bdquery/z?d106:h.r.00010:>

FTC Report on Self-regulation and Privacy Online recommends no
legislation at this time
http://www.ftc.gov/opa/1999/9907/report1999.htm

House Commerce Committee Telecommunications, Trade and Consumer
Protection Subcommittee July 13 hearing - Current status of privacy
protections for online consumers
http://www.house.gov/commerce/

House Banking Committee Financial Institutions Subcommittee will hold
hearings on financial privacy July 20-21
http://www.house.gov/banking/71399rou.htm

U.S. Bancorp and State of Minnesota settle lawsuit over transfer of
customer information
http://www.ag.state.mn.us/home/files/news/pr_usbank_07011999.html

Joint release on the EU-US data protection dialogue
http://europa.eu.int/comm/dg15/en/media/dataprot/news/239.htm 

Direct Marketing Association "Privacy Promise" requirement for members
http://www.the-dma.org/texis/scripts/news/newspaper/+mr+em5-0wBm+emE7vwwwr/d
isplayArticle.html

Mary Clare Fitzgerald "Electronic Commerce - Policing only the
'Submarginal fringe'"
http://192.41.52.91/electron/submarginal.html

Privacy and American Business
http://www.pandab.org

B. ONLINE BANKING AND PAYMENTS 

Electronic Financial Services Council (advocacy group founded by
Microsoft, Intuit, Countrywide and GE Capital)
http://www.efscouncil.com/  

OCC approves formation of online bank by Canadian Imperial Bank of
Commerce, which will market its services primarily through retail
store kiosks
http://www.occ.treas.gov/interp/jul99/ca313.pdf

Financial Services Technology Consortium projects under formation -
draft paper on Moving ATMs to the Internet and description of the FAST
(Financial Agent Secured Transactions) concept
http://www.fstc.org/projects/fast/index.html

MasterCard announces introduction of rules for Internet casinos (an
interesting example of how the payments system may get entangled with
the issue of the validity of online transactions) 
http://www.mastercard.com/about/press/990709a.html

Federal Reserve Bank of New York Current Issues in Economics and
Finance, Vol. 5 No. 8,  Cecchetti,  "The Future of Financial
Intermediation and Regulation"
http://www.ny.frb.org/rmaghome/curr_iss/ci5-8.html

Federal Reserve Bank of New York Economic Policy Review, Vol.5 No. 2
Radecki, "Banks' Payments-driven Revenues"
http://www.ny.frb.org/rmaghome/econ_pol/799rad.htm

Jane Kaufman Winn, draft "Electronic Chattel Paper under Article 9:
Updating the Concept of Embodied Rights for Electronic Commerce"
http://www.smu.edu/~jwinn/winnecp.htm

PriceWaterhouseCoopers - Summary of Report on Creating Tomorrow's
Retail Bank
http://www.pricewaterhousecoopers.com/gx/eng/ins-sol/spec-int/tlrb/index.html

Financial Crimes Enforcement Center issues SAR Bulletin on use of ATMs
for money laundering
http://www.treas.gov/fincen/sarbul1.html

Accredited Standards Committee X12 Finance Subcommittee (X12F)
(develops EDI transaction sets for financial transactions)
http://www.disa.org/x12/x12f/

http://www.civics.com/content/s761-markup.htm

CNET News story on Banking Industry Technology Secretariat security lab

Identrus announces pilot of Business-to-Business trust network
http://www.identrus.com/news.html

Ernst & Young 1998 Global Information Security Survey

Materials from joint OECD-private sector workshop on electronic

International Organization for Standardization/International

JTC1 Subcommittee 27 - Security Techniques

United States Postal Service Information Based Indicia Program for

D. SECURITIES TRADING, OFFERINGS AND SETTLEMENT

Senate Banking Committee Chairman and members submit recommendations
for inclusion in forthcoming Securities Markets Enhancement Act
(including provisions regarding online investing)
http://www.senate.gov/~banking/docs/reports/smea.htm

SEC announces formation of Working Group on After-hours Trading
http://www.sec.gov/offices/marketr/aftrhrs3.htm

U.S. District Court for the District of Columbia issues decision
in Taucher v. Born.  The court holds on First Amendment grounds
that the requirement to register as Commodities Trading Advisors
is unconstitutional as applied to plaintiffs, who publish online
information and analysis on commodities trading
http://www.ij.org/cftc/

SEC issues no-action letter to Wit Capital that should facilitate
individual investor participation in IPOs
http://www.witcapital.com/press/pr_33.html

International Organization for Standardization May 1999 press release
on new securities message standards
http://www.iso.ch/presse/safeguar.htm

Department of Commerce report, The Emerging Digital Economy II
http://www.ecommerce.gov/ede/

1st draft of "Standard for Internet Commerce" best practices released
for comment.  The Standard was developed by a group of experts
sponsored by ZD Net and others 
http://www.gii.com/standard/index.html

Nine Internet industry leaders form NetCoalition for public policy
advocacy
http://www.netcoalition.com/

Ernst & Young study on loss of sales tax revenues from Internet
commerce, "The Sky is not Falling"
http://www.ey.com/ecommerce/sky.asp

OECD Workshop on Business-to-Business electronic commerce, June 17th
http://www.oecd.org/dsti/sti/it/ec/act/oslo_workshop.htm

Papers from the Second Berlin Internet Economics Workshop, including
papers on electronic money, pricing and auctions
http://www.berlecon.de/iew2/program.html

Asia Pacific Economic Cooperation E-commerce steering group
http://www.ecommerce.gov/apec/

Federal Reserve Bank of New York Current Issues in Economics and
Finance, Vol.5 No.10,  Wenninger, "Business-to-Business Electronic

House and Senate pass H.R. 775, the Y2K Act, expected to be signed by

<http://thomas.loc.gov/cgi-bin/bdquery/z?d106:h.r.00775:>

E-conomy project conference papers
