# postscript from Joe Bates

```
Date: Fri, 20 May 94 01:03:26 -0400
From: Joseph_Bates@WIZARD.OZ.CS.CMU.EDU
Subject: Bates on CBN's interview of Bates

For my friends and interested others, I'd like to comment very briefly
on the events leading up to my appearance on CBN earlier this week.

I have no affiliation with CBN.  I have some general familiarity with
their activities.  A number of months ago the Carnegie Mellon public
relations office asked me to do an interview with a reporter from CBN.
After some hesitation, I agreed.  The interview took place in a CMU
office.  The interviewer was exceptionally polite and friendly....
astoundingly so.  We discussed the Oz project's work on believable
agents, and we looked at the Woggles.  The reporter understood the
work rather well.  I was told the interview would be reported as 
news, in particular as an introduction to an area of science and
intellectual inquiry.  I didn't see the broadcast piece, and so leave
it to you to decide if this was so.

It has been educational to me that this piece seems to have attracted
more attention than work presented at AAAI and CogSci conferences, 
in archival journals, and in popular sources such as Science News,
Newsweek, and newspapers.  Maybe we should change the spin of our
conference papers.

Joe
```
