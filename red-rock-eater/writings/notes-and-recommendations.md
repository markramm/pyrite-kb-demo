### Notes and recommendations.

Ever since I ceased publication of [The Network Observer](tno.html)
in the summer of 1996, I have been using the [Red Rock
Eater News Service](rre.html) to send out occasional batches of informal "notes and
recommendations". Here I've gathered links to these messages in the Web-based
[RRE archives](https://web.archive.org/web/20240629224130/http://commons.somewhere.com/rre/)
that are kindly maintained by Kee Hinckley
<[nazgul@somewhere.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20240629224130/mailto:nazgul@somewhere.com)> at
[Somewhere.Com](https://web.archive.org/web/20240629224130/http://www.somewhere.com/). I've also tried to
summarize the topics they cover. Most of them end with lists of useful URL's
that RRE subscribers have sent me.
