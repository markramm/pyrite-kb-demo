2.  Democracy Notes Monthly Newsletter - DO-NOTES@TC.UMN.EDU

---

The Democracy Notes e-mail newsletter is the primary communication
vehicle for the those interested and involved with the convergence of
democracy and the Internet.  

Whether its an event from UK Citizens Online Democracy, a community
networking conference in Australia, the release of the G7 Government
Online and Democracy White Paper, an announcement from Minnesota 
E-Democracy, an example post from Internet-based advocacy activities, 
or related commercial and online media initiatives, joining Democracy 
Notes will be your way to stay informed, but not overwhelmed by 
e-mail.  

It is the newsletter to which you should submit your project and 
conference announcements, research articles and news URLs, and 
other important online resources.  Send submissions to: 
DO-NOTES@TC.UMN.EDU 

DO-NOTES will be produced once a month in a short update style.
Please join today, this service will start once we reach 1000 direct
subscribers.  The DO-WIRE service is now open as described below.

To SUBSCRIBE, please send an e-mail message to:

                 LISTSERV@TC.UMN.EDU

In the body of your message, write:

                 SUBSCRIBE DO-NOTES "Your Name (Place)"

Please note that you will be asked to confirm your subscription
via e-mail.

3.  Democracies Online Newswire - DO-WIRE@TC.UMN.EDU - ** NOW OPEN **

---

DO-WIRE is a moderated e-mail announcement list covering the
convergence of democracies and the Internet around the world.  
Relevant "best of" posts will be forwarded from the civic, 
political, government, media, and private sectors on new
projects, events, online resources, research, conferences, as
well as references to important news stories.

Democracies Online promotes the development and sustainability of
online civic participation and democracy efforts around the world
through experience, outreach, and education.  The number of messages
per week range from 5 to 10. All posts are archived from:  
     http://www.e-democracy.org/do 

To SUBSCRIBE, please send an e-mail message to:

                 LISTSERV@TC.UMN.EDU

In the body of your message, write:

                 SUBSCRIBE DO-WIRE "Your Name (Place)"

Please note that you will be asked to confirm your subscription
via e-mail.

DO-WIRE Submission Information

Submissions to the Democracies Online Newswire are encouraged.
Future details will be made available on posts with the scope 
of the service.  Please send your proposed submissions to:
   DO-WIRE@TC.UMN.EDU

Submissions to DO-WIRE will be considered for direct distribution on
the Newswire and for inclusion in the periodic Democracy Notes
newsletter.
