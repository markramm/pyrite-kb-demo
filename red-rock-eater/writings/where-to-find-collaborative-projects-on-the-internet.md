As more and more schools are connecting to the Internet, teachers find
themselves searching for appropriate on-line projects to integrate into
the required coursework.

Although the Global SchoolNet Foundation (GSN) and other organizations
including I*EARN, NASA, IECC, KIDLINK, GLOBE, Academy One, TIES, Tenet,
TERC, and several universities offer many excellent projects each year,
there is no one central location to find and plan for these projects ahead
of time. Too often teachers hear of a project only after it is already in
progress, not allowing them enough time to conduct appropriate pre-project
activities.

Global SchoolNet has taken the initiative to make planning for projects a
bit easier, by registering projects on a monthly calendar.

There are already dozens of projects registered from September 95 through
May 96.

---

If you would like to help out, and you know of projects that are open to
outside participants, please send a message to:

   proj.register@gsn.org

Make sure the message INCLUDES the following information:

- Month and year project BEGINS

- How long the project will last (approx.)

- Name of project

- The email address for the contact person

- A one-two sentence project description

- OPTIONAL: The URL where potential participants
  can find additional information

GSN will list this information in a matrix divided by the month in which
the project begins.

The matrix will be accessible via the WWWeb and distributed via the
HILITES mailing list.
