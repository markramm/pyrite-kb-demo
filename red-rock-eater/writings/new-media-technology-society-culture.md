# New Media: Technology, Society, Culture

```
Date: Thu, 10 Aug 1995 03:35:02 -0300
From: Makoff, Jane <makoff@sageltd.co.uk>
To: Multiple recipients of list <contemporanea@ufba.br>
Subject: [CONTEMPORANEA:44] Call for Papers

     New Media: Technology, Society, Culture
     
     The International Journal of Research on New Information and 
     Communication Technologies
     
     New Media: Technology, Society, Culture is being launched to provide a 
     forum for social sciences, cultural studies and innovation and policy 
     studies perspectives on the emergence, adoption and implications of 
     new information and communication technologies, and their applications 
     in the form of new media products and services.
     
     Themes to be addressed include:
     
     The specific characteristics and implications of the new media, 
     technologies and services
     
     Socio-economic, cultural and political factors shaping the emergence, 
     application, diffusion and use of the new ICTs
     
     Patterns of processes of demand, consumption and use of new/innovative 
     technologies, media and cultural forms
     
     Critical studies of regulatory and other public policy implications of 
     new media innovations
     
     Contending and conflicting approaches to key issues and concepts
     
     
     The journal is being edited by Paschal Preston from Dublin City 
     University and will be published by SAGE Publications from July 1996.
     
     If you are interested in contributing you should contact:
     
     Dr Paschal Preston
     Director, COMTEC
     Dublin City University
     Glasnevin
     Dublin 9
     Ireland
     e-mail: PrestonP@dcu.ie
     
     Further information on the journal is available from Jane Makoff, SAGE 
     Publications at <makoff@sageltd.co.uk>.
```
