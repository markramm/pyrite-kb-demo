europemedia.com is a free weekly news report covering the main developments
in the European online markets.

March 16 - 22:
Hello, in a week where Europe didn't always come up smelling of roses,
the EU's Fifth Framework Programme commited to the internet - so
progress there. Plus British Telecom and its European partners has
launched a new fibre network. Bulgaria gets a new portal, and the
privacy seal programme now has a presence in Europe. Germany is still
not happy with cookies, and a "spot the shamrock" online promotion
brought a little joy to St Patrick's Day. Delight, a new Italian web
browser rises like Venus in a Renaisance painting, and Digilog moves HQ
from Norway to Switzerland. The Third Russian Internet forum took place
and half of Sweden - really - owns a PC. Three new mobile news systems
have launched in Switzerland and a new internet access system using ADSL
is progressing in Spain. More next week, let's get this network built -
there's not long to the next century!
