# McLibel Support Campaign

```
Date: Sat, 12 Aug 1995 10:08:45 -0400
From: dbriars@world.std.com
To: US McLibel Support Campaign <mclibel@world.std.com>
Subject: Recent News Coverage

Besides the recent Wall Street Journal (July 18)and New York Times (Aug 6)
pieces, there are other indications that the McLibel campaign is creeping into
the mainstream media in sometimes strange places.

The "E" entertainment channel on cable/satellite aired a snippet on their
evening gossip show about how Geoffrey Giordano, an early Ronald McDonald,
had made a public apology that he felt guilty about duping children and that
he had become a vegetarian. (Aug 9, 1995)

NBC New York TV News show "Inside Edition" did a show on how McDonald's was
displacing the famous art/freak show which had been a fixture on Coney Island
for many years, and which had added much character and humor to the community.
"It's strictly a business decision" was a quote prominently inserted. (Aug
11,1995)
```
