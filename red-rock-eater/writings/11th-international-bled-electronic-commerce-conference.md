	11th International Bled Electronic Commerce Conference
	   "ELECTRONIC COMMERCE IN THE INFORMATION SOCIETY"

		   Bled, Slovenia - June 8-10, 1998

This is the premier event in the EC calendar.  It comprises:

-   a fully-refereed, multi-stream Research Track, focussing on Electronic
    Commerce, EDI and Inter-Organisational Systems, which attracts close to
    100 specialist academics and doctoral candidates;

-   a Graduate Students Consortium and Educational Symposium, to be held in
    Otocec, a castle on the river Krka, on June 11-12 1998;

-   an Industry Track led by eminent keynote speakers, which attracts
    business leaders from Europe, North America and the Asia-Pacific;

-   pre-conference meetings and tutorials;  and

-   an Exhibition of Information Technology for Electronic Commerce.

Details are at:
http://ecom.fovref.uni-mb.si

These pages provide details about:
-   how to submit a paper;
-   how to apply for entry to the Consortium and Symposium;
-   how to plan your travel to Slovenia;  and
-   who to contact with enquiries.

The Conference is held each year at the beautiful alpine village of Bled,
30 km south of the Austrian border.  It is sponsored by the University of
Maribor's Faculty of Organizational Sciences, Slovenia's Electronic
Commerce Project, the Ministry of Science and Technology of the Republic of
Slovenia, and the Chamber of Economy of Slovenia
