# The Canadian Committee to Protect Journalists

```
Date: Sun, 6 Mar 1994 11:15:56 -0500
From: Robin Rowland <eridani@IO.ORG>
To: Multiple recipients of list CARR-L <CARR-L@ulkyvm.bitnet>
Subject: Committee to Protect Journalists

The Canadian Committee to Protect Journalists, which is the world
wide clearing house for information about journalists under threat
is online at

ccpj@web.apc.org

"Now that the CCPJ is operating the clearing house, we have probably
the most detailed and most up-to-date information in the world
on press freedom issues,' says Clearing House Manager Gabrielle
Iribarne. "We get this information by e-mail and fax from groups
all over just as soon as they learn of it themselves."

The CCPJ is willing to share information with news organizations,
journalism schools, human rights groups, academics and the public
when they need background information concerning attacks on the press
around the world.
```
