# Democracy in Mexico

```
Date: Fri, 24 Feb 1995 22:15:44 -0800
From: National Commission for Democracy in Mexico <moonlight@igc2.igc.apc.org>
Subject: Mail from NCDM

Dear Friends:

A moment to catch our breath and make a suggestion.  Here at
the national office we are trying hard to get the latest
information from Mexico translated into English and out into
the internet world.  We really want everyone to have access
to this information and in the beginning we tried to email
to at least everyone who is not on Peacenet.  However the
volume of work is increasing everyday, and we are still
learning how to most effectively use our technology. 

Therefore we have an urgent plea to improve our
communication process. There is a service on internet, 
which is available to anyone on internet.  It's called
chiapas-l.  It's the main source of information in internet
about Chiapas.  

We post everything to chiapas-l as well as to reg.mexico in
peacenet.  If you subscribe to chiapas-l, all of the
postings will come automatically to your mailbox.

To subscribe to chiapas-l, send a message to:
majordomo@profmexis.dgsca.unam.mx

The message should say:  subscribe chiapas-l <your full email
address>

In addition, if you want to get La Jornada in Spanish, you
can subscribe to Notipaez. To do so, send a message to:
listproc@listas.unam.mx

The message should say: subscribe notipaez <your full email
address>.

If you do that, you will immediately subscribe and can then
receive all of our messages automatically as well as many
others from across the world.

We are sorry that we can not continue mailing to everyone
individually, but in some ways it is a good sign--the
movement is growing.

Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions.

Cindy, volunteer National Commission for Democracy in Mexico
```
