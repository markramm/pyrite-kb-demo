Technology and Jobs

Excerpt from Technology and Jobs: Forging a New Social Contract? :

The issues evolving from the impact of technology on jobs and 
work have always been complex and require an analysis, not only 
of the roots of problems resulting from technology, but also 
including the policy choices that might exist Q including imagining 
bold new ideas for social organization that might free up the 
potential of new technologies. 

The issue is so fundamental that it would be foolhardy to refuse to 
take up this challenge of analysis, imagining, and creating. In this 
issue of The CPSR Newsletter, we begin to respond to this 
challenge.
