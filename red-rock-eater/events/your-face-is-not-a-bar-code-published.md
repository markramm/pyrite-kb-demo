Agre publishes 'Your Face Is Not a Bar Code: Arguments Against Automatic Face Recognition in Public Places.' The essay applies the capture model to facial recognition technology, arguing against its deployment in public spaces. Published just before September 11th, the essay's arguments become even more urgent in the post-9/11 security environment.
