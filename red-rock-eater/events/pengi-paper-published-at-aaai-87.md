Agre and David Chapman present 'Pengi: An Implementation of a Theory of Activity' at the Sixth National Conference on Artificial Intelligence. The paper demonstrates that complex behavior can emerge from deictic representations without classical planning, marking Agre's first major public break with the dominant AI paradigm.
