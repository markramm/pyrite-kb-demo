## Overview

Agre's transitional institutional home, approximately 1992-1996. UCSD's strength in cognitive science and its interdisciplinary culture supported Agre's shift from AI systems work toward critical theory of technology. This is where he wrote 'Surveillance and Capture,' began articulating critical technical practice, and launched the Red Rock Eater News Service.
