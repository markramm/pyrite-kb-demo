## Overview

Agre's email newsletter, published from 1993 to approximately 2003. RRE was one of the most influential intellectual forums of the early internet, combining curated links to articles and reports with Agre's own essays on technology, politics, institutions, and intellectual practice.

At its peak, RRE reached thousands of subscribers including academics, technologists, journalists, and policy advocates. The newsletter modeled a distinctive form of public intellectual engagement — neither academic journal nor mass media, but something in between: a curated, opinionated, deeply thoughtful stream of ideas circulated through email.

The name comes from a formation in Sedona, Arizona.
