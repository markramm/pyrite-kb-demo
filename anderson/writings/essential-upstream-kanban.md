"Essential Upstream Kanban" (2019) is a condensed free guide to upstream kanban practices, paralleling the role that [[essential-kanban-condensed]] plays for the main [[kanban-method]]. It provides an accessible entry point to the upstream kanban concepts developed more fully in [[upstream-kanban]].

## Purpose

The guide distills the core upstream kanban practices into a concise, practitioner-oriented reference. It covers how to visualize the flow of options through a discovery workflow, how to apply WIP limits to upstream work, and how to use explicit policies to govern when options advance from discovery to commitment for development.

The free download model mirrors the approach taken with [[essential-kanban-condensed]]: by making the condensed guide freely available, [[lean-kanban-inc]] and [[kanban-university]] lower the barrier to engagement with upstream kanban concepts while preserving the training and certification programs as the primary commercial offering.

## Position in the upstream kanban literature

"Essential Upstream Kanban" and [[upstream-kanban]] together form the upstream kanban literature in Anderson's bibliography. The condensed guide serves practitioners who need a quick orientation; the full book provides the theoretical grounding, case studies, and operational detail needed for sustained implementation.

Both works represent the [[maturity-and-enterprise-era]] expansion of Anderson's work beyond delivery-focused Kanban into the complete service delivery lifecycle, from discovery of what to build through delivery of working software.
