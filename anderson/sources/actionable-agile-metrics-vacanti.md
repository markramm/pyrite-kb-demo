*Actionable Agile Metrics for Predictability* (2015) by [[dan-vacanti]] is the definitive guide to flow-based metrics for Kanban and Agile teams. Vacanti, who helped develop the [[kanban-method]] at [[corbis]] alongside Anderson and [[corey-ladas]], drew on his deep experience with flow systems to create a comprehensive framework for using cycle time, throughput, and work-in-progress data to achieve predictability.

The book extended Anderson's [[manage-flow]] and [[fitness-criteria]] concepts into a rigorous analytical framework, providing the measurement foundation that the [[kanban-method]] needed for data-driven improvement. Vacanti later helped develop the "Professional Scrum with Kanban" class with Scrum.org (2017), bridging the two communities.
