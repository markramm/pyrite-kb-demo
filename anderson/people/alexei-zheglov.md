Alexei Zheglov is a software consultant and co-creator, with Anderson, of the Fit for Purpose framework documented in [[fit-for-purpose]]. The framework extends the [[kanban-method]] into product management territory, providing [[fitness-criteria]] — metrics for evaluating whether a service meets customer expectations — as the bridge between flow management and business outcomes.

Zheglov's contribution to the [[maturity-and-enterprise-era]] of Anderson's work reflects the method's evolution from a team-level flow management tool toward a comprehensive approach to organizational fitness and customer value delivery.
