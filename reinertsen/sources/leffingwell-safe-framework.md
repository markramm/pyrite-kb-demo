[[dean-leffingwell]]'s book that laid groundwork for SAFe ([[scaled-agile-inc]]). Incorporates Reinertsen's [[weighted-shortest-job-first]] and [[cost-of-delay]] as core prioritization mechanisms. Documents how Reinertsen's economic framework was adapted for enterprise-scale agile adoption — a significant channel of influence, though one that simplified some of Reinertsen's nuance for broader accessibility.
