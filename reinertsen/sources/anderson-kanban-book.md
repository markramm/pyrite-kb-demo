[[david-anderson]]'s foundational text on the Kanban Method for knowledge work. Anderson explicitly credits Reinertsen's [[queueing-theory-applied]], [[wip-constraints]], and [[batch-size-reduction]] as theoretical foundations. The book demonstrates how Reinertsen's principles translate into practical team-level practices — WIP limits, pull systems, and explicit policies. A key document of Reinertsen's influence on software development methodology.
