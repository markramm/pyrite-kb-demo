Publication of [[managing-the-design-factory]], Reinertsen's breakthrough work applying [[queueing-theory-applied]] and [[economic-framework-for-prioritization]] to product development. This book introduced the [[u-curve-optimization]] pattern and made the rigorous economic case for practices that lean practitioners had adopted intuitively. It became a key reference for [[mary-poppendieck]] and [[tom-poppendieck]] as they developed lean software development.
