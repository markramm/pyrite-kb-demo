Publication of [[principles-of-product-development-flow]], Reinertsen's magnum opus. Organized 175 principles across economics, queueing, variability, batch size, WIP, flow control, sequencing, and [[decentralized-control]]. Coined "second generation lean product development" to distinguish his economic approach from mechanical application of manufacturing lean tools. This work directly influenced [[david-anderson]]'s Kanban Method and [[dean-leffingwell]]'s SAFe framework.
