Donald Reinertsen's consulting firm, based in Redondo Beach, California. Through this practice, Reinertsen has advised product development organizations on applying [[economic-framework-for-prioritization]], [[queueing-theory-applied]], and [[batch-size-reduction]] to their development processes. The consulting work provided the empirical grounding for his books — many examples in [[managing-the-design-factory]] and [[principles-of-product-development-flow]] come from client engagements.
