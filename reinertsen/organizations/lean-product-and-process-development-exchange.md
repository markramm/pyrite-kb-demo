Annual conference focused on lean product development practices. Reinertsen has been a featured speaker, presenting on [[economic-framework-for-prioritization]], [[queueing-theory-applied]], and the distinction between first-generation and second-generation lean product development. The conference community represents practitioners actively applying Reinertsen's frameworks alongside Toyota-derived lean practices.
