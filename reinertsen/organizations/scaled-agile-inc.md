Company behind the Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe), founded by [[dean-leffingwell]]. SAFe is the most widely adopted enterprise agile framework and incorporates several of Reinertsen's concepts, most notably [[weighted-shortest-job-first]] for backlog prioritization and [[cost-of-delay]] as an economic input. SAFe's adoption brought Reinertsen's ideas to a mass enterprise audience, though Reinertsen's original formulations are more nuanced than SAFe's standardized implementations.
