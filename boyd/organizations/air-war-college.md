The Air War College at Maxwell AFB is the Air Force's senior professional military education institution. Professor Grant T. Hammond of the Air War College became one of Boyd's most important academic interpreters, eventually editing Boyd's collected briefings for publication by Air University Press in 2018 as "[[hammond-discourse-compilation|A Discourse on Winning and Losing]]." This posthumous publication — more than two decades after Boyd's death — finally made Boyd's briefing materials available in a single, citable volume. The irony was not lost on Boyd's acolytes: the Air Force institution that had marginalized Boyd during his career became the publisher that preserved his intellectual legacy.
