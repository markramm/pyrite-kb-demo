Grant T. Hammond's eulogy for [[john-boyd|John Boyd]], delivered at his funeral at Arlington National Cemetery on March 11, 1997. Hammond, a professor at the Air War College who later edited Boyd's collected briefings as [[discourse-on-winning-and-losing|"A Discourse on Winning and Losing"]] for Air University Press, provides a personal and intellectual tribute that captures both Boyd's brilliance and his difficult personal qualities. A valuable primary source for understanding how Boyd was perceived by those closest to his intellectual project. See also [[final-years]] for the context of Boyd's death.
