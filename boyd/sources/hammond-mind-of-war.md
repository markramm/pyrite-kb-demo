An academic treatment of [[john-boyd|Boyd's]] intellectual contributions by Grant T. Hammond, a professor at the Air War College. More focused on Boyd's ideas and their intellectual context than [[coram-boyd-biography|Coram's]] biographical narrative, "The Mind of War" provides deeper analysis of Boyd's strategic theories — including the [[ooda-loop|OODA loop]] and [[destruction-and-creation-concept|Destruction and Creation]] — and their relationship to military thought.

Hammond later edited the Air University Press compilation of Boyd's briefings, [[discourse-on-winning-and-losing|"A Discourse on Winning and Losing"]] (2018), making the complete collection available in print for the first time.
