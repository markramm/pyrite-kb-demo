A personal and analytical essay by Chuck Spinney, one of Boyd's closest acolytes, reflecting on the epistemological framework laid out in "Destruction and Creation." Spinney brings both personal knowledge of Boyd's thinking process and his own analytical background to bear on the essay's argument about the dialectic of mental model destruction and creation.
