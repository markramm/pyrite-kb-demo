Summer of Protocols is a research program on protocol design launched in 2023, directed by Venkatesh Rao and co-led with [[tim-beiko]] of the [[ethereum-foundation]], which provided the program's funding. It commissioned a cohort of researchers from diverse disciplinary backgrounds to investigate protocols — formal and informal coordination rules — as a fundamental phenomenon of human social organization. The [[summer-of-protocols-launch]] event marks the beginning of what became one of the most substantive institutional projects of Rao's [[post-ribbonfarm]] career.

## Program design and scope

Summer of Protocols was explicitly interdisciplinary and humanistic in orientation, resisting the tendency of protocol research in the blockchain context to reduce to technical or financial questions. The program commissioned fellows from backgrounds including philosophy, anthropology, design, computer science, political theory, and literary studies to investigate protocols at multiple scales: internet protocols, legal protocols, social rituals, biological coordination mechanisms, and the Ethereum protocol as a specific case study.

Rao's directorship of the program reflected his positioning as the intellectual figure within the broader Web3 ecosystem who had most seriously developed [[protocol-thinking]] as a general framework. The program's design embodied his claim, articulated in [[unreasonable-sufficiency-of-protocols]], that protocols are undertheorized as a category and that genuine protocol design requires humanistic as much as technical understanding.

## Intellectual output

The Summer of Protocols produced a substantial body of published research — essays, reports, and analyses by participating fellows — that collectively constitute the most extensive elaboration of [[protocol-thinking]] as a research program. The program's output expanded Rao's own thinking about protocols: his [[unreasonable-sufficiency-of-protocols]] essay, developed in connection with the program, represents the most systematic statement of his protocol philosophy.

The program also connected Rao to a new intellectual community — researchers thinking seriously about protocols from diverse disciplinary perspectives — that extended the [[cozyweb]] network of high-trust intellectual exchange beyond the Ribbonfarm community.

## Significance in Rao's intellectual biography

Summer of Protocols represents a distinctive model for Rao's post-Ribbonfarm intellectual production: institution-sponsored, time-bounded, collaborative, and focused on a specific intellectual question rather than the open-ended essay format of Ribbonfarm. The program demonstrates Rao's capacity to function not just as a solo writer but as a research director and intellectual convener — a different kind of intellectual productivity that the [[post-ribbonfarm]] era has enabled.

The program's connection to the [[ethereum-foundation]] situates it within the Web3 intellectual ecosystem while deliberately expanding beyond it, using blockchain sponsorship to fund genuinely broad humanistic inquiry into coordination and protocol design.
