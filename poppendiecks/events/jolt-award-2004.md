[[lean-software-development-agile-toolkit-2003]] won the 2004 Jolt Software Development Productivity Award, one of the most prestigious recognitions in software development publishing. The award validated the lean software approach within the practitioner community and significantly boosted the book's visibility. Coming just one year after publication, the Jolt Award signaled that the Agile community recognized lean software as a substantial intellectual contribution, not just another methodology book.
