An author Q&A published by InfoQ around the launch of [[the-lean-mindset-2013]]. The Poppendiecks discuss the book's core argument that lean is a mindset rather than a set of practices, their case study methodology, and how their thinking evolved since the original trilogy. They recommend John Shook's "Managing to Learn" as a complementary text, connecting their work to the broader lean management education tradition.
