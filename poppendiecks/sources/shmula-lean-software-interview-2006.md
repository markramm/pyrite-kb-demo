An early interview with [[mary-poppendieck]] on the Shmula lean blog, conducted around the time of [[implementing-lean-software-development-2006]]. Notable for capturing Mary's views during the middle of the trilogy period, when lean software was gaining traction but before it was absorbed into mainstream Agile practice. She emphasizes applying lean thinking principles rather than copying manufacturing practices — a distinction that would become central to [[the-lean-mindset-2013]].
