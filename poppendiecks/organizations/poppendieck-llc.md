Poppendieck LLC is the consulting practice through which [[mary-poppendieck]] and [[tom-poppendieck]] delivered lean software development training, workshops, and organizational consulting. It is the vehicle through which the ideas developed in their trilogy — [[lean-software-development-agile-toolkit-2003]], [[implementing-lean-software-development-2006]], and [[leading-lean-software-development-2009]] — were applied in real organizations.

## Function

The consulting practice served two roles simultaneously. It was the commercial vehicle for the Poppendiecks' professional work, and it was a source of ongoing field material that informed successive refinements of their framework. Organizational engagements revealed where the lean software translation held under real conditions and where it required adjustment — particularly on the leadership and cultural dimensions that became central to [[leading-lean-software-development-2009]].

## Context

Poppendieck LLC operated during the period when lean software was establishing itself as a recognized complement to Scrum and XP. The [[agile-alliance]] conference circuit provided visibility; the consulting practice provided depth of engagement. Together they gave the Poppendiecks both reach and grounding that purely academic or purely practitioner work would not have provided.
