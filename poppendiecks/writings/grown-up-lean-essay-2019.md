One of the most popular essays on [[lean-essays-blog]], "Grown-Up Lean" examines how lean thinking has matured since the Poppendiecks first introduced it to software. Reflects on what "grown-up" application of lean principles looks like after two decades of adoption — moving past mechanical application of practices toward genuine internalization of [[lean-mindset|lean mindset]] principles. Published 16 years after [[lean-software-development-agile-toolkit-2003]], it represents [[mary-poppendieck]]'s mature perspective on the movement she helped create.
