Xlibris is a self-publishing platform and print-on-demand publisher. Its significance to the Richards KB is narrow but concrete: it is the publisher of [[certain-to-win]], Richards's primary book-length application of [[john-boyd]]'s strategic framework to business management.

The choice of Xlibris for [[certain-to-win]] reflects the [[certain-to-win-publication]] context: the book emerged from a community of practitioners and enthusiasts rather than from a commercial publishing pipeline, and self-publishing allowed Richards to reach that niche audience without the delays and commercial pressures of traditional publication. The Boyd community had a tradition of distributing ideas outside normal channels — Boyd himself never published a book — and the Xlibris publication of [[certain-to-win]] continues that tradition in a civilian context.

The self-published nature of [[certain-to-win]] has both limited and shaped its reception: it circulates primarily within communities already oriented toward Boyd (the [[boyd-and-beyond-conference]] world, the [[agile-alliance]] periphery) rather than through mainstream business book distribution. This contrasts with the wider reach of [[robert-coram]]'s commercially published Boyd biography.
