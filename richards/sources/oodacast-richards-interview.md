## Overview

OODAcast Episode 31 features an extended interview with Richards conducted by Matt Devost and Bob Gourley. The OODAcast is a podcast produced by OODA LLC, a consultancy whose name and orientation are drawn directly from [[john-boyd]]'s strategic framework. The episode covers Boyd's ideas, the creative process behind the OODA loop, and the application of [[ooda-based-competition]] to the business world.

## Content

Richards discusses his direct relationship with [[john-boyd]], including the origins of their collaboration and his role in constructing the first graphics of the OODA loop from Boyd's sketches. The interview covers the intellectual history of the OODA loop — how it developed, what Boyd intended by its individual components, and how it has been misunderstood and simplified in popular use.

The business applications discussion connects to the themes of [[certain-to-win]] and the [[certain-to-win-framework]], drawing the line from Boyd's military strategic theory through Richards' management translation to practical competitive strategy. Richards addresses common misconceptions about the OODA loop, consistent with the corrective work he did in [[boyds-real-ooda-loop]].

## Value as Source

The interview is particularly useful for understanding Richards' account of the OODA loop's origins and his personal relationship with Boyd. As one of the few surviving members of the [[boyd-circle-period]] inner circle who worked directly with Boyd on core conceptual materials, Richards' testimony on these matters is primary-source evidence. The podcast format allows for extended discussion of nuances that shorter written pieces do not accommodate.

The episode complements [[sonshi-richards-interview]] and [[no-way-out-podcast-richards]] as part of the interview record that documents Richards' thinking across different periods and audiences.
